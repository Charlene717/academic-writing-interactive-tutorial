故事開場

場景：從 50,000 篇到 30 篇——Alex 的 3 週覺悟

SCENE · 2024

當搜尋框打出「TNBC immunotherapy」，跳出 50,000 筆

研究生 Alex 接到第一個任務：為實驗室寫一篇關於三陰性乳癌 (TNBC) 免疫治療的 systematic review。她興奮地打開 PubMed，輸入 TNBC immunotherapy，按下 Enter——50,247 筆結果。她楞住了。

第一週，她下載前 200 篇 PDF 開始讀。第二週發現自己讀的論文大多是無關的——有的是 review、有的是 case report、有的是動物實驗、有的是 2005 年的舊綜述。她寄信給 PI 陳教授：「我快被淹沒了。」回信只有兩個字：「策略？」

第三週，Alex 終於打開《Cochrane Handbook》，學會兩件事：(1) MeSH terms ≠ 自由文字，要兩者並用；(2) 一個 systematic review 必須有事先註冊的篩選準則。她重寫查詢：("Triple Negative Breast Neoplasms"[Mesh] OR "TNBC"[tiab]) AND ("Immunotherapy"[Mesh] OR pembrolizumab[tiab] OR atezolizumab[tiab]) AND "Randomized Controlled Trial"[ptyp] AND 2018:2024[dp]。返回 387 篇。

她接著用 Connected Papers 上傳 3 篇 anchor papers，視覺化共引網路；再用 Research Rabbit 自動推薦延伸文獻。一天之內，她鎖定了真正關鍵的 30 篇 seminal papers——其中 5 篇是原始 PubMed 字串漏掉的，因為作者用了「checkpoint inhibitor」而非「immunotherapy」。陳教授看完她的 PRISMA flow diagram，只回了一句：「下週可以開始寫了。」

Graduate student Alex gets her first big assignment: write a systematic review on triple-negative breast cancer (TNBC) immunotherapy. She excitedly opens PubMed, types TNBC immunotherapy, hits Enter — 50,247 results. She freezes.

Week one she downloads the first 200 PDFs and starts reading. Week two she realises most of them are irrelevant — some are reviews, some case reports, some animal studies, some 2005 commentary. She emails her PI Prof. Chen: "I'm drowning." The reply is one word: "Strategy?"

Week three, Alex finally opens the Cochrane Handbook and learns two things: (1) MeSH terms ≠ free text — use both; (2) a systematic review needs pre-registered inclusion criteria. She rewrites her query: ("Triple Negative Breast Neoplasms"[Mesh] OR "TNBC"[tiab]) AND ("Immunotherapy"[Mesh] OR pembrolizumab[tiab] OR atezolizumab[tiab]) AND "Randomized Controlled Trial"[ptyp] AND 2018:2024[dp]. Returns 387 hits.

Then she uploads 3 anchor papers to Connected Papers to visualise the co-citation graph, and uses Research Rabbit to auto-recommend related work. Within a single day she identifies the 30 truly seminal papers — 5 of which her original PubMed string had missed, because the authors used "checkpoint inhibitor" instead of "immunotherapy." Prof. Chen reads her PRISMA flow diagram and replies: "You can start writing next week."

核心詞彙

12 個關鍵術語（雙版本）

每個詞有兩個版本：🧒「給小孩看」用比喻、📐「給研究者看」給定義。

Each term has two versions: 🧒 "for kids" uses a metaphor; 📐 "for researchers" gives the formal definition.

PRISMA 2020
PRISMA 2020 規範
寫 systematic review 的「食譜」——每一步都要寫清楚，別人才能照做出同樣的菜。
A "recipe" for systematic reviews — write every step clearly so others can reproduce it.
Preferred Reporting Items for Systematic Reviews and Meta-Analyses 2020 update (Page et al. 2021 BMJ)。27 項 checklist + 流程圖；取代 2009 版；要求報告自動化工具、registration ID、報告偏誤。
Preferred Reporting Items for Systematic Reviews and Meta-Analyses 2020 update (Page et al. 2021 BMJ). 27-item checklist + flow diagram; replaces 2009; requires reporting automation tools, registration ID, reporting bias.
Systematic Review
系統性回顧
用「全世界都認可的步驟」把某個問題的所有相關研究找齊、評斷、整合——不能漏、不能偏心。
Find every relevant study on a question using globally accepted steps — no gaps, no favouritism.
回答 effectiveness 問題的窮盡式合成；遵循 PRISMA + 預先註冊 (PROSPERO)；至少 3 個資料庫 + 兩人獨立篩選 + 品質評估 (RoB 2 / ROBINS-I)。
Exhaustive synthesis answering effectiveness questions; follows PRISMA + pre-registration (PROSPERO); ≥3 databases + dual independent screening + quality appraisal (RoB 2 / ROBINS-I).
Scoping Review
範疇式回顧
畫一張「這個領域目前有什麼」的地圖——不是評誰最好，而是先搞清楚地形。
Draw a map of "what already exists" in a field — not judging quality, just charting the terrain.
JBI / Arksey-O'Malley 2005 方法學；回答 landscape 問題；不評品質、不做 meta-analysis；報告規範採 PRISMA-ScR (Tricco 2018 Ann Intern Med)。
JBI / Arksey-O'Malley 2005 methodology; answers landscape questions; no quality appraisal, no meta-analysis; reported per PRISMA-ScR (Tricco 2018 Ann Intern Med).
Narrative Review
敘事型回顧
領域專家「講故事」的綜述——觀點豐富但較容易偏心，因為沒固定流程。
A "storytelling" review by a domain expert — rich perspective but more prone to bias since there's no fixed process.
傳統綜述；無預先註冊、無窮盡檢索；選擇偏誤高、不可複製；常用於 thesis Chapter 2、commentary、textbook。
Traditional review; no pre-registration, no exhaustive search; high selection bias, not replicable; common in thesis Ch. 2, commentaries, textbooks.
MeSH term
MeSH 醫學主題詞
圖書館員幫每篇論文貼的「主題標籤」——你用標籤搜尋，不會被作者用不同字眼害你漏掉。
"Topic stickers" librarians attach to every paper — search by sticker, never miss a synonym.
Medical Subject Headings；NLM 自 1960 年起維護的受控詞彙樹；29,000+ terms；每年更新；PubMed indexer 為每篇加 10–15 個 MeSH；查詢用 "Term"[Mesh]。
Medical Subject Headings; NLM-maintained controlled vocabulary tree since 1960; 29,000+ terms; updated yearly; PubMed indexers tag 10–15 MeSH per article; query with "Term"[Mesh].
Boolean operator
布林運算子
三個搜尋魔法字：AND（兩個都要）、OR（任一即可）、NOT（不要這個）。
Three search spells: AND (need both), OR (either works), NOT (exclude this).
由 George Boole 1854 提出；AND = 交集 (縮小)、OR = 聯集 (擴大)、NOT = 差集 (慎用，易漏文獻)。括號控制優先序：(A OR B) AND C。
From George Boole 1854; AND = intersection (narrow), OR = union (broaden), NOT = difference (use cautiously, drops papers). Parentheses control precedence: (A OR B) AND C.
PROSPERO
PROSPERO 註冊庫
在做 review 之前，先把「我要做什麼」公開貼出來——之後不能偷偷改。
Before starting a review, publicly post "what I'll do" — you can't quietly change it later.
International Prospective Register of Systematic Reviews；University of York CRD 2011 上線；免費；PRISMA 2020 要求填註冊 ID 或解釋未註冊原因；2024 累計 >250,000 註冊。
International Prospective Register of Systematic Reviews; launched 2011 by University of York CRD; free; PRISMA 2020 requires registration ID or explanation; >250,000 registrations by 2024.
Cochrane
Cochrane 協作組織
全球 systematic review 的「最高標準學校」——它的 review 被當成醫學決策的金本位。
The "gold-standard school" of systematic reviews — its outputs are treated as the bar for medical decisions.
Cochrane Collaboration (1993 創立)；50+ Cochrane Review Groups；發行 Cochrane Library；維護 Cochrane Handbook (current v6.5, 2024) 與 RoB 2 工具；對 LLM 篩選有 2025 官方 guidance。
Cochrane Collaboration (founded 1993); 50+ Review Groups; publishes Cochrane Library; maintains Cochrane Handbook (v6.5, 2024) and RoB 2 tool; issued 2025 LLM-screening guidance.
Connected Papers
Connected Papers 共引網路圖
輸入 1 篇種子論文，它幫你畫出「跟這篇最相關的 50 篇」星圖——很快找到該領域中心。
Input 1 seed paper, it draws a star map of "the 50 most related papers" — fast way to find a field's centre.
Eitan & Tarnavsky 2020 上線；基於 co-citation + bibliographic coupling；不是時序而是主題相似度；PRISMA 中只能列為 supplementary search。Litmaps、Inciteful、Research Rabbit 為同類工具。
Launched 2020 by Eitan & Tarnavsky; based on co-citation + bibliographic coupling; clusters by topic, not time; in PRISMA counts only as supplementary search. Sister tools: Litmaps, Inciteful, Research Rabbit.
Forward citation
前向引用追蹤
找出「引用了這篇論文的所有後續研究」——順著時間往前走，找最新進展。
Find every later study that cites this paper — walk forward in time to catch the latest extensions.
backward = 讀 reference list；forward = 看 Web of Science / Scopus / Google Scholar 的「Cited by」。PRISMA-S (Rethlefsen 2021) 要求 SR 必須報告 forward citation chasing 是否執行。
Backward = read reference list; forward = use "Cited by" on Web of Science / Scopus / Google Scholar. PRISMA-S (Rethlefsen 2021) requires SRs to report whether forward citation chasing was done.
Cochrane RoB 2
Cochrane 偏誤風險 2 工具
5 個面向的「研究品質檢查表」——告訴你每篇 RCT 的結果有多可信。
A 5-domain "study-quality checklist" — tells you how much to trust each RCT's findings.
Sterne et al. 2019 BMJ；RCT 專用；5 domains: randomisation / deviations from intended interventions / missing outcome data / measurement / selection of reported result；each = Low / Some concerns / High。觀察性研究改用 ROBINS-I。
Sterne et al. 2019 BMJ; RCT-specific; 5 domains: randomisation / deviations / missing outcome data / measurement / selection of reported result; each rated Low / Some concerns / High. Observational studies use ROBINS-I.
GRADE
GRADE 證據確定性分級
把整個 systematic review 的證據強度評成 ⭐⭐⭐⭐「高 / 中 / 低 / 極低」——讓臨床醫師知道能多放心。
Rate the overall evidence strength of a systematic review as ⭐⭐⭐⭐ "High / Moderate / Low / Very Low" — so clinicians know how confident to be.
Grading of Recommendations Assessment, Development and Evaluation；Guyatt 2008 BMJ；五個降級 (risk of bias, inconsistency, indirectness, imprecision, publication bias) + 三個升級 (large effect, dose-response, plausible confounders)；PRISMA 2020 建議使用。
Grading of Recommendations Assessment, Development and Evaluation; Guyatt 2008 BMJ; 5 downgrading factors (risk of bias, inconsistency, indirectness, imprecision, publication bias) + 3 upgrading (large effect, dose-response, plausible confounders); recommended by PRISMA 2020.

一個比喻
🏗️
比喻 · 建築工地

Literature review 像建築工地：你站在哪個鋼樑上，決定能蓋多高。每一篇被你引用的文獻就是一根鋼樑——歪了、鏽了、或根本不是承重結構，你的論文就在風中搖晃。Narrative review 像隨手堆磚塊；systematic review 像 ISO 認證的鋼骨工法；scoping review 像 site survey 報告，告訴你「這塊地能蓋什麼」。PRISMA 2020 是 building code，PROSPERO 是建照申請。沒有 footing 的高樓終究會塌。

A literature review is a construction site: which steel beam you stand on decides how high you can build. Every citation is a beam — bent, rusted, or non-load-bearing, and your paper sways in the wind. A narrative review is a casual brick pile; a systematic review is ISO-certified steel framework; a scoping review is a site-survey report telling you "what can be built here." PRISMA 2020 is the building code; PROSPERO is the permit application. Towers without footings collapse — eventually.

Σ 推導

從 keyword 到 PRISMA flow diagram 的 8 步

Σ Search-to-Screen Pipeline
Σ₁

定義 PICO inclusion / exclusion。把研究問題拆解成可被執行的篩選條件：人群 / 介入 / 對照 / 結果 / 研究設計 / 年代範圍。寫成 protocol 文件而非僅在腦袋裡。

Define PICO inclusion/exclusion. Decompose the research question into executable screening criteria: population / intervention / comparator / outcome / design / year range. Put it in a protocol document, not just in your head.

Σ₂

到 PROSPERO 註冊 protocol。Systematic review 在 2024–26 期刊投稿幾乎強制；註冊時間戳就是抗 HARKing 的盾牌。

Register at PROSPERO. For SRs in 2024–26 this is virtually mandatory at submission. The timestamp is your HARKing shield.

Σ₃

建立 PubMed query：MeSH + Boolean。用「MeSH 受控詞彙 + 自由文字 [tiab]」配合 AND / OR / NOT 串接；高敏感性 (sensitivity) > 高特異性 (specificity) 為原則。

Build the PubMed query: MeSH + Boolean. Combine MeSH controlled vocabulary with free-text [tiab] using AND / OR / NOT; prioritize sensitivity over specificity.

Σ₄

橫跨 PubMed / Embase / Web of Science / Scopus / Cochrane 執行檢索。SR 至少 3 個資料庫；同一個 query 在各庫的語法要客製化 (Emtree vs MeSH)。記錄每庫的日期與結果數。

Run across PubMed / Embase / Web of Science / Scopus / Cochrane. SRs need ≥3 databases; the query must be customized per database (Emtree vs MeSH). Log search date and hit count per source.

Σ₅

用 Zotero 去重 (dedup)。把五個來源的 RIS / BibTeX 匯入 Zotero（或 EndNote / Mendeley），重複文獻會自動標記。典型可去掉 25–40% 重複。

Dedup in Zotero. Import RIS / BibTeX from all five sources into Zotero (or EndNote / Mendeley) — duplicates are auto-flagged. Typically removes 25–40%.

Σ₆

Title / abstract screening (Rayyan 雙人盲審)。兩位審稿人獨立標記 include / exclude / maybe；Cohen's κ ≥0.6 才能往下走。Rayyan AI 可以加速但人類雙盲仍是金標準。

Title/abstract screening (Rayyan, dual-blind). Two reviewers independently tag include / exclude / maybe; require Cohen's κ ≥0.6 before proceeding. Rayyan AI accelerates, but dual-blind human review remains the gold standard.

Σ₇

Full-text screening。把 maybe + include 的全文找來，再次雙人盲審；無法取得的全文也要記錄。每篇排除都需在 PRISMA 圖標註原因。

Full-text screening. Retrieve full text for include + maybe, run a second dual-blind round; log unretrievable papers. Every exclusion needs a documented reason in the PRISMA diagram.

Σ₈

產出 PRISMA 2020 flow diagram。Identification → Screening → Eligibility → Included 四階段，每階段標 N 與排除原因。flow diagram = 你的 audit trail。

Generate the PRISMA 2020 flow diagram. Four stages — Identification → Screening → Eligibility → Included — each with N and exclusion reasons. The diagram is your audit trail.

思辨

三個值得吵的問題

爭點 1

Systematic review 在 AI 時代還需要嗎?

傳統派：AI 工具沒有 protocol、沒有 inter-rater check、沒有 risk-of-bias assessment——它做的是「綜述」不是「systematic review」。
Traditionalists: AI tools have no protocol, no inter-rater check, no risk-of-bias appraisal — they produce "summaries", not "systematic reviews".
加速派：2025 Cochrane 已正式允許 LLM 協助 abstract screening；reviewer 角色從「掃讀者」變「驗證者」，總時程可從 18 個月壓縮到 6。
Accelerationists: Cochrane 2025 officially permits LLM-assisted abstract screening; reviewers shift from "skimmers" to "verifiers", compressing 18 months down to 6.
政策視角：NICE、WHO、FDA 仍只接受 PRISMA-compliant SR 作為 guideline evidence；AI 摘要尚未獲 regulatory acceptance。
Policy view: NICE, WHO, FDA still accept only PRISMA-compliant SRs as guideline-grade evidence; AI summaries lack regulatory acceptance.
爭點 2

Living review 是否成主流?

支持派：新證據出現 6 個月內必須更新，否則 guideline 永遠落後 18–36 個月。Living review + LLM auto-screening 是必然趨勢。
Supporters: Guidelines must update within 6 months of new evidence or fall 18–36 months behind. Living reviews + LLM screening are inevitable.
謹慎派：Living = 永遠 in-flight；版本管理、citation 穩定性、reviewer fatigue 都是未解問題。
Skeptics: "Living" means perpetually in-flight; version control, citation stability, reviewer fatigue remain unsolved.
資金視角：Living review 經費模式從「一次性 grant」變「subscription」——這是學術出版的結構性轉變。
Funding view: Living reviews shift funding from "one-shot grants" to "subscriptions" — a structural transformation of scholarly publishing.
爭點 3

Citation chasing vs structured search — 哪個重要?

結構化派：PRISMA / Cochrane 強調可複製性；snowballing 是黑盒子，無法在 method section 寫清楚。
Structuralists: PRISMA / Cochrane prize reproducibility; snowballing is a black box that can't be cleanly written into Methods.
實務派：關鍵 grey literature（preprints、政策文件、conference proceedings）只靠 structured query 抓不到；citation chasing 是必要的補洞工具。
Practitioners: Crucial grey literature (preprints, policy reports, conference proceedings) escapes structured queries; citation chasing fills the gaps.
2024–26 折衷：Research Rabbit / Connected Papers / Litmaps 把 citation network 視覺化——把 snowballing 變得可複製、可記錄。
2024–26 middle ground: Research Rabbit / Connected Papers / Litmaps visualize the citation network, making snowballing reproducible and loggable.
個案聚焦

一個典範、一個警訊

🏆
典範 · Cochrane 抗生素抗藥性回顧

Cochrane Library 在「短期抗生素療程是否導致抗藥性」的 systematic review 是典範實踐：PROSPERO pre-registered、跨 6 個資料庫、雙人盲審、Cohen's κ ≥0.78、ROBINS-I + RoB 2.0 雙工具評估 bias、GRADE certainty rating、PRISMA 2020 flow diagram 完整、forest plot + heterogeneity I² 全數報告。這篇被 WHO 列入 antimicrobial stewardship guideline。教訓：systematic review 不是「我看了很多論文」而是「我做了一個 audit-able 的證據合成」。

Cochrane Library's SR on "short antibiotic courses and resistance" is exemplary: PROSPERO pre-registration, 6-database search, dual-blind screening, Cohen's κ ≥0.78, dual-tool bias assessment (ROBINS-I + RoB 2.0), GRADE certainty rating, full PRISMA 2020 flow diagram, forest plots with I² heterogeneity throughout. WHO adopted it into antimicrobial stewardship guidance. Lesson: a systematic review isn't "I read many papers" — it's "I performed an auditable evidence synthesis".

⚠️
警訊 · Wakefield 1998 Lancet

1998 Andrew Wakefield 在 Lancet 發表的 MMR 疫苗—自閉症 case series，名義上是 case series 但被當成 narrative literature review 引用，引發全球反疫苗運動延燒 20 年。後續查證：N=12、無對照、利益衝突未揭露、資料造假。2010 Lancet 正式撤稿；Wakefield 醫師資格被吊銷。教訓：(1) Narrative review 缺乏 protocol，極易被個人偏好或利益污染；(2) 期刊撤稿後的災難式 citation chain 在 retracted-watch 時代仍會繼續傳播——所以引文清單必須定期回查 Retraction Watch。

Andrew Wakefield's 1998 MMR vaccine–autism case series in the Lancet — nominally a case series but cited as if it were a narrative literature review — fueled a global anti-vaccine movement for 20 years. Subsequent investigation: N=12, no controls, undisclosed conflict of interest, data fabrication. Retracted by the Lancet in 2010; Wakefield was struck off the medical register. Lessons: (1) narrative reviews without protocol are easily contaminated by preference or interest; (2) retracted-citation chains keep spreading even in the Retraction-Watch era — every citation list must be re-audited.

常見陷阱

六個會毀掉文獻回顧的錯誤

① 只搜尋 PubMed

PubMed 是必要不是充分。SR 需要 ≥3 個資料庫；只用 PubMed 會漏 Embase 獨有的歐洲藥物文獻、漏 WoS / Scopus 的跨領域引用、漏 Cochrane 的臨床試驗。

PubMed is necessary but not sufficient. SRs need ≥3 databases. Relying on PubMed alone misses Embase's European drug literature, WoS / Scopus cross-disciplinary citations, and Cochrane's trial registry.

② 跳過 PROSPERO 註冊

2024–26 高影響期刊要求 SR 在資料蒐集前 PROSPERO 註冊；事後註冊或不註冊 = HARKing 嫌疑，審稿人會直接 desk-reject。

High-impact journals in 2024–26 require pre-data-collection PROSPERO registration. Post-hoc or no registration = HARKing suspicion → desk-reject.

③ 沒有 inter-rater check

單人篩選 → 無法保證 reproducibility；雙人盲審 + Cohen's κ ≥0.6 是 SR 基本門檻。κ 沒寫就是缺項。

Single-reviewer screening → no reproducibility guarantee. Dual-blind + Cohen's κ ≥0.6 is the SR floor. No κ reported = a missing item.

④ 只引用自家 group

論文 ref 80% 都是 corresponding author 的舊作 → reviewer 立刻警覺 narcissistic citation。應主動找 critique 你立場的論文並引用。

If 80% of refs are the corresponding author's prior work → reviewers flag narcissistic citation. Actively cite papers that critique your stance.

⑤ 混用 narrative + systematic protocols

標題寫「systematic review」但方法寫「我們選了相關論文」——這是方法學上的災難。要嘛全 PRISMA，要嘛誠實寫 narrative。

Title says "systematic review" but Methods says "we selected relevant papers" — a methodological disaster. Either fully PRISMA, or honestly write "narrative".

⑥ 忘了 Cochrane 資料萃取表

SR 的 data extraction 應使用標準化表格（Cochrane 模板或 EPHPP）：自由文字 = 不可複製。雙人萃取後互相核對。

SR data extraction must use a standardized form (Cochrane template or EPHPP): free text = irreproducible. Dual extraction with cross-check.

壞例 vs 好例

同一段文獻回顧，兩種寫法

❌ 壞範例

「許多研究指出許多現象 [1–15]。」

"Several studies have shown many things [1–15]."

  • 塊狀引用 (block citation) 15 篇——無法判斷你讀了哪幾篇
  • A 15-paper block citation — no way to tell which you actually read
  • 「許多」「現象」皆無具體內容
  • "Several" and "things" carry zero specific content
  • 無方法、無 gap、無批判
  • No methodology, no gap, no critique
  • 看不出研究問題如何接續既有文獻
  • No logical link to your research question
  • 審稿人 30 秒判定「沒讀文獻」
  • Reviewer concludes "didn't read the literature" in 30 s

✅ 好範例

「三個 TNBC scRNA-seq atlases (Wu et al. 2021 Nat Genet；Bassez et al. 2021 Nat Med；Liu et al. 2023 Cell) 已系統性刻劃 inter-tumoral heterogeneity，但 post-chemotherapy 的 longitudinal cell-state shift 尚未被刻劃。本研究透過 paired pre/post pembrolizumab 的時序 scRNA-seq 填補此 gap。」

"Three scRNA-seq atlases of TNBC (Wu et al. 2021 Nat Genet; Bassez et al. 2021 Nat Med; Liu et al. 2023 Cell) have systematically characterized inter-tumoral heterogeneity, yet longitudinal post-chemotherapy cell-state dynamics remain uncharacterized. This study fills this gap via paired pre/post pembrolizumab scRNA-seq."

  • 具體 author + year + journal，每篇可被檢核
  • Specific author + year + journal — each citation verifiable
  • 區分「已被回答」與「尚未被回答」
  • Separates "answered" from "unanswered"
  • 明確 gap：post-chemo longitudinal cell-state
  • Explicit gap: post-chemo longitudinal cell-state
  • 告訴 reviewer 你的研究如何填補 gap
  • Tells the reviewer exactly how your study fills it
  • 3 篇精選 > 15 篇 block citation
  • 3 curated citations > a 15-paper block

2024–2026 進階

前沿議題（可摺疊）

SOTA
(a) Rayyan + Covidence 自動化 2025

Rayyan AI (2024 update) 加入 LLM-assisted abstract prediction：上傳 ~100 篇人類標記訓練集，工具會自動為剩餘數千篇預測 include / exclude 機率，reviewer 從「掃 5000 篇」變「驗證 top-confidence 600 篇」。Covidence 2025 整合 risk-of-bias 自動評分（GRADE）+ 引文連結 + PRISMA flow auto-render。實務經驗：

  • 仍需人類雙盲對 high-confidence 預測做 spot-check（建議抽 20%）。
  • LLM 對非英文摘要 (CJK / 阿拉伯文) 預測準確率仍偏低 (~70%)。
  • Cochrane 2025 LLM-assisted SR guidance：必須在 Methods 揭露 model name + version + prompt + accuracy 評估。

Rayyan AI (2024 update) added LLM-assisted abstract prediction: upload ~100 human-labelled examples and the tool predicts include/exclude probabilities for thousands more — reviewers shift from "skim 5000" to "verify the top-confidence 600". Covidence 2025 integrates auto risk-of-bias scoring (GRADE), citation linking, and PRISMA-flow auto-rendering. Practical notes:

  • Dual-blind human spot-checks are still required on high-confidence predictions (recommend 20%).
  • LLM accuracy on non-English abstracts (CJK / Arabic) remains modest (~70%).
  • Cochrane 2025 LLM-assisted SR guidance: Methods must disclose model name + version + prompt + accuracy assessment.
(b) Living systematic reviews

Living SR 的核心是「持續更新而非一次出版」：用自動 query agents (PubMed RSS / NCBI eutils webhook) 監測新文獻，當納入標準觸發時自動加入並重跑 meta-analysis。代表案例：

  • COVID-NMA (Boutron et al., Cochrane)：COVID-19 治療 living network meta-analysis，每週更新。
  • Living Evidence for Diabetes Consortium：糖尿病臨床指引 living review。
  • Annals of Internal Medicine 2024 起設立 "Living Reviews" 專欄。

挑戰：版本管理 (DOI 是否需要 minor-version？)、reviewer fatigue、citation 穩定性。NLM 2025 開始為 living review 提供 versioned DOI。

The core of a living SR is "continuous update rather than one-shot publication": automated query agents (PubMed RSS / NCBI eutils webhook) monitor new literature and auto-incorporate it on trigger, re-running the meta-analysis. Flagship examples:

  • COVID-NMA (Boutron et al., Cochrane): weekly-updated living network meta-analysis of COVID-19 therapies.
  • Living Evidence for Diabetes Consortium: living review of diabetes clinical guidance.
  • Annals of Internal Medicine launched a "Living Reviews" section in 2024.

Challenges: versioning (does each minor revision need a new DOI?), reviewer fatigue, citation stability. NLM 2025 began issuing versioned DOIs for living reviews.

(c) AI 文獻搜尋工具 — Elicit / Scite / Consensus 2024–26

三大新興工具的角色定位：

  • Elicit (2024+)：自然語言問題 → 自動結構化抽取 (PICO / sample size / effect size) 的「結構化合成」。適合 scoping review 第一輪。
  • Scite：把每個 citation 分類為 supporting / contrasting / mentioning，協助找出真正被質疑的論文。在 risk-of-bias 評估上特別有用。
  • Consensus.app：基於 Semantic Scholar 200M+ 論文資料庫，自動產生 "yes / no / mixed" 投票式 summary。風險：oversimplification + hallucination。

三者皆不取代 PRISMA SR，但都是 narrative review、scoping review、grant writing 的加速器。Cochrane 2025 立場：可作 supplementary tool，主流程仍須人類雙盲。

Roles of the three emerging tools:

  • Elicit (2024+): natural-language question → auto-extracted structured fields (PICO / sample size / effect size) = "structured synthesis". Suits the first pass of a scoping review.
  • Scite: classifies each citation as supporting / contrasting / mentioning — helps surface contested papers. Particularly useful for risk-of-bias.
  • Consensus.app: built on Semantic Scholar's 200M+ corpus; produces "yes / no / mixed" vote-style summaries. Risk: oversimplification + hallucination.

None replaces PRISMA SR, but all accelerate narrative reviews, scoping reviews, and grant writing. Cochrane 2025 stance: acceptable as supplementary tools, but the main pipeline still requires dual-blind human review.

概覽

為什麼文獻回顧是論文的根基？

多數初學者把文獻回顧當成「交代有人做過什麼」——這是錯的。文獻回顧的真正目的是建構研究 gap、證明你的研究是 novel 的、並展現方法學上的判斷力。一個好的文獻回顧 = 一個有說服力的 Introduction。

從審稿人的角度看，文獻回顧揭露三件事：(1) 你是否真的讀懂該領域？(2) 你能否批判而非僅羅列？(3) 你的研究問題在邏輯上是否真的接續既有文獻？這三題若答不出來，再漂亮的數據都救不了論文。

Beginners often treat the literature review as "listing who did what." That's wrong. The real purpose is to construct the research gap, demonstrate that your work is novel, and show methodological judgment. A strong literature review equals a persuasive Introduction.

From a reviewer's perspective, a literature review answers three questions: (1) Do you actually understand the field? (2) Can you critique rather than merely list? (3) Does your research question logically follow from prior work? If these are unanswered, no amount of pretty data will save the paper.

💡
核心原則：不要寫「A 做了 X、B 做了 Y、C 做了 Z」的流水帳。寫「在 X 已被回答之後，仍存在 Y 的爭議；本研究透過 Z 嘗試解決」。文獻回顧的最終句永遠要導出 your research gap。 Core principle: Don't write "A did X, B did Y, C did Z." Write "After X was answered, Y remained contested; this study attempts to resolve it via Z." Every literature review must end by pointing to your research gap.
三類回顧

一、三種文獻回顧類型

並非所有「回顧」都長一樣。選錯類型，意味方法學標準、報告規範、可投稿期刊的範圍都不同。

Not all "reviews" are the same. Choosing the wrong type means different methodological standards, reporting guidelines, and target journals.

📖

Narrative Review

傳統敘事型，由領域專家依個人視角綜合既有研究。範圍廣、無正式 protocol、不要求窮盡式檢索。
適用：論文 Chapter 2、短篇 commentary、教科書章節。
限制：選擇偏誤高、不可複製。

The traditional form: domain experts synthesize prior work from their perspective. Broad scope, no formal protocol, no exhaustive search required.
Use for: Thesis Chapter 2, short commentary, textbook chapters.
Limits: High selection bias; not replicable.

🔬

Systematic Review

遵循 PRISMA 2020 protocol，預先註冊（PROSPERO）、窮盡式檢索、可複製。常與 meta-analysis 搭配做量化合成。
適用：臨床證據合成、藥物療效評估、Cochrane review。
限制：耗時 6–18 個月、需多人協作。

Follows the PRISMA 2020 protocol — pre-registered (PROSPERO), exhaustive search, fully replicable. Often paired with meta-analysis for quantitative synthesis.
Use for: Clinical evidence synthesis, drug efficacy, Cochrane reviews.
Limits: 6–18 months; requires a team.

🗺️

Scoping Review

遵循 JBI / Arksey-O'Malley 方法學，目的是繪製領域地圖——回答「目前做了什麼？」而非「X 有沒有效？」。
適用：新興領域、概念尚未統一、需識別研究 gap。
限制：不做品質評估、不做量化合成。

Follows JBI / Arksey-O'Malley methodology to map the landscape — answers "what's been done?" not "does X work?"
Use for: Emerging fields, unsettled concepts, identifying gaps.
Limits: No quality appraisal; no quantitative synthesis.

⚠️
常見誤用：把 narrative review 寫成「我選了我喜歡的 30 篇」、把 systematic review 寫成「我搜尋了 PubMed」（缺其他資料庫）、把 scoping review 寫成 systematic review 卻沒做品質評估。選定類型 = 接受該類型的全部報告規範。
Common misuse: Writing a narrative review as "the 30 papers I liked"; calling something a systematic review when only PubMed was searched (no other databases); labeling a scoping review as systematic but skipping quality appraisal. Choosing a type means accepting its full reporting guideline.
檢索策略

二、PubMed / MeSH 檢索策略

檢索能力決定你能找到什麼證據。關鍵是：MeSH terms（受控詞彙）+ 自由文字（free text, [tiab]）的組合，並用 Boolean 運算子 (AND / OR / NOT) 串接。

主要資料庫：PubMed（生醫主力）、Embase（歐洲藥物文獻較全）、Web of Science（跨領域、引用追蹤）、Scopus（與 WoS 互補）、Cochrane（臨床試驗）、Google Scholar（補遺、找灰文獻）。Systematic review 通常要求至少 3 個資料庫。

Search skill determines what evidence you can find. The key: combine MeSH terms (controlled vocabulary) with free text ([tiab]), joined by Boolean operators (AND / OR / NOT).

Main databases: PubMed (biomedical mainstay), Embase (European drug literature), Web of Science (cross-discipline + citation tracing), Scopus (complements WoS), Cochrane (clinical trials), Google Scholar (gap-filling + grey literature). Systematic reviews typically require at least 3 databases.

PRISMA 流程

三、PRISMA 2020 流程

PRISMA (Preferred Reporting Items for Systematic Reviews and Meta-Analyses) 是 systematic review / meta-analysis 的國際報告規範。2020 版取代 2009 版，加入了報告偏誤、自動化工具、註冊資訊等要求。

PRISMA (Preferred Reporting Items for Systematic Reviews and Meta-Analyses) is the international reporting standard. The 2020 version replaces the 2009 version, adding requirements for reporting bias, automation tools, and registration.

1

Identification

從資料庫(PubMed, Embase, Cochrane…) 與其他來源(citation chasing、會議論文、灰文獻、領域專家推薦) 撈出所有可能相關的紀錄。
需報告：每個資料庫命中數、去除重複後數量、自動化工具篩除的數量。

Pull all potentially relevant records from databases (PubMed, Embase, Cochrane…) and other sources (citation chasing, conference papers, grey literature, expert referrals).
Report: hits per database, number after de-duplication, records removed by automation tools.

2

Screening

標題 + 摘要篩選。兩位獨立審查者依預先定義的 inclusion / exclusion criteria 進行；不一致時由第三人仲裁或討論共識。
需報告：篩除數量與主要排除原因（如：非人類研究、非 RCT、不符合 PICO）。

Title + abstract screening. Two independent reviewers apply pre-specified inclusion / exclusion criteria; disagreements are resolved by a third reviewer or consensus.
Report: number excluded and main reasons (e.g., non-human, non-RCT, off-PICO).

3

Eligibility (Full-text)

對通過 screening 的論文取得全文閱讀，依 criteria 判斷是否納入。
需報告：每篇被排除的論文 ID + 排除理由（這是 PRISMA 的硬性要求，常被忽略）。

Retrieve full text for screened-in papers and judge eligibility against the criteria.
Report: ID + reason for every excluded paper at this stage (a hard PRISMA requirement, often skipped).

4

Included

最終納入合成的研究數量。可進一步分為「納入質性合成」與「納入量化合成 (meta-analysis)」兩個子數字。
需報告：總納入數、研究特徵摘要表 (Table 1)、品質評估 (RoB 2 / ROBINS-I / Newcastle-Ottawa)。

Final number of studies included for synthesis. Often split into "included in qualitative synthesis" and "included in quantitative synthesis (meta-analysis)."
Report: total included, study characteristics table (Table 1), quality appraisal (RoB 2 / ROBINS-I / Newcastle-Ottawa).

引用追蹤

四、引用追蹤與 citation tools

關鍵字檢索常會漏掉領域核心論文（搜尋詞不一致、新概念尚未進入 MeSH）。引用追蹤 (citation chasing) 是 PRISMA 認可的補充方法。

Keyword search often misses seminal papers (vocabulary mismatch, new concepts not yet in MeSH). Citation chasing is a PRISMA-recognized complement.

🌳 找到該領域所有核心論文的流程

Q1:
已找到 1 篇與你研究最接近的「種子論文」嗎？→ 否 → 先在 PubMed 用最具體關鍵字找出 1–3 篇 anchor papers。
Q2:
做後向檢索 (backward)：讀種子論文的 reference list，挑出反覆被引用的經典文獻。
Q3:
做前向檢索 (forward)：在 Web of Science / Google Scholar 看「Cited by」，找出近年延伸研究。
Q4:
用視覺化工具一次跳網絡：Connected Papers（共引網路圖）、Research Rabbit（自動推薦）、Inciteful（多輸入論文聯合分析）、Citation Gecko（種子論文輻射）、Litmaps（時序圖）。
Q5:
反覆 Q2–Q4 直到「無新論文出現」 (theoretical saturation)，即視為達到完整覆蓋。
Q1:
Have you found one "seed paper" closest to your study? → No → Use the most specific keywords on PubMed to identify 1–3 anchor papers first.
Q2:
Run backward search: read the seed paper's reference list and pick out frequently-cited classics.
Q3:
Run forward search: use "Cited by" on Web of Science / Google Scholar to find recent extensions.
Q4:
Jump the citation network with visual tools: Connected Papers (co-citation graph), Research Rabbit (auto-recommend), Inciteful (multi-input analysis), Citation Gecko (seed-paper radial), Litmaps (timeline view).
Q5:
Iterate Q2–Q4 until "no new papers appear" (theoretical saturation) — that's full coverage.
⚠️
注意：視覺化工具（Connected Papers 等）方便但不可取代資料庫檢索。在 systematic review 中只能作為輔助 (supplementary search) 報告，不能取代 PubMed/Embase 的主檢索字串。
Note: Visual tools (Connected Papers, etc.) are convenient but cannot replace database searches. In systematic reviews they count only as supplementary search, never as a substitute for the main PubMed/Embase strategy.
範例對照

五、好壞文獻回顧句型對照

❌ 空洞泛論

「許多研究顯示，腸道菌與肥胖有關。」
(「許多研究」=多少？指誰？讀者要憑空相信。)

"Many studies have shown that gut microbiota is related to obesity."
("Many" = how many? Whom? The reader is asked to take it on faith.)

✅ 具體有出處

「自 Turnbaugh 等人 (2006, Nature) 首次報告肥胖小鼠的 Firmicutes/Bacteroidetes 比例升高以來，至少 14 項人體研究 (n > 50) 重複了此關聯，但 Sze & Schloss (2016) 的 meta-analysis 指出此關聯在跨族群分析中並不穩健 [3,7,12]。」

"Since Turnbaugh et al. (2006, Nature) first reported elevated Firmicutes/Bacteroidetes ratios in obese mice, at least 14 human studies (n > 50) have replicated this association; however, the meta-analysis by Sze & Schloss (2016) showed it does not hold across populations [3,7,12]."

❌ 流水帳列舉

「Smith (2018) 做了 X。Lee (2019) 做了 Y。Wang (2020) 做了 Z。」
(三位作者並列，沒整合、沒批判、沒指出 gap。)

"Smith (2018) did X. Lee (2019) did Y. Wang (2020) did Z."
(Three names listed in parallel — no synthesis, no critique, no gap identified.)

✅ 整合 + 指出 gap

「儘管 Smith (2018) 與 Lee (2019) 在體外系統證實 X 蛋白質參與細胞遷移，Wang (2020) 在小鼠模型中卻未能重現此現象——三項研究皆未在人類原代細胞檢驗，本研究即填補此空缺。」

"Although Smith (2018) and Lee (2019) showed in vitro that protein X drives cell migration, Wang (2020) failed to reproduce this in mouse models. None of the three tested human primary cells — a gap this study addresses."

❌ 過度依賴單一來源

「根據 Wikipedia / 一篇 2005 年的綜述…」
(Wikipedia 不是學術引用；10 年前的綜述在快速領域已過時。)

"According to Wikipedia / a 2005 review…"
(Wikipedia is not a citable source; a 10-year-old review is outdated in fast-moving fields.)

✅ 近期同儕審查文獻

「最新的綜述 (Garcia 等人, 2024, Nat Rev Genet) 整合了 2018–2023 年間 87 項 scRNA-seq 研究，指出 cell-type annotation 一致性仍低於 70%。」

"The most recent review (Garcia et al., 2024, Nat Rev Genet) synthesized 87 scRNA-seq studies (2018–2023) and reported cell-type annotation concordance below 70%."

歷史軼事 · Archie Cochrane 與 systematic review 革命 (1972 → 1993)。蘇格蘭流行病學家 Archie Cochrane 於 1972 年出版《Effectiveness and Efficiency: Random Reflections on Health Services》，對當時臨床醫學提出震撼性批評：「沒有任何方法可以系統性地把所有 RCT 證據整合給醫師使用——醫學在用 anecdote 治病。」這段話被視為 evidence-based medicine 的奠基宣言。20 年後，1993 年 Cochrane Collaboration 在牛津正式成立，目標就是按系統化方法回答 Cochrane 的批評。如今 Cochrane Library 收錄 9,000+ 篇 SR，每年被 WHO / NICE / NIH 引用作為 guideline evidence。教訓：一段 1972 年的牢騷，最終改變了全球臨床醫學的證據標準——但耗時 21 年才從 manifesto 變成 institution。

Historical anecdote · Archie Cochrane and the systematic-review revolution (1972 → 1993). Scottish epidemiologist Archie Cochrane published Effectiveness and Efficiency: Random Reflections on Health Services in 1972, with a thunderclap critique of clinical medicine: "there is no systematic way to bring all RCT evidence to clinicians — medicine is practiced by anecdote." This passage is widely regarded as the founding manifesto of evidence-based medicine. Twenty-one years later, the Cochrane Collaboration was formally founded in Oxford in 1993, dedicated to answering that critique systematically. Today the Cochrane Library houses 9,000+ SRs, cited annually by WHO / NICE / NIH as guideline-grade evidence. Lesson: a single 1972 complaint reshaped global clinical evidence standards — but it took 21 years to go from manifesto to institution.
