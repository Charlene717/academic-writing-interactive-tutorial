故事開場

場景：Yuki 與她的第一篇 paper figures

SCENE · 2025 · Kyoto Univ.

從「bar + SEM」到「boxplot + jitter」的覺醒

博士生 Yuki 第一次準備自己的 paper figures，做了 12 張漂亮的 bar chart 加 SEM 誤差棒，每張都閃著鮮豔的紅綠對比色。她把 PDF 交給指導教授 佐藤老師，內心滿是期待。隔天會議室裡，老師只說一句：「不要用 bar + SEM。換成 boxplot + jitter，原始點都畫出來。」

Yuki 一開始很不服氣——bar + SEM 不就是大家都在用的嗎？老師沒多解釋，丟給她一篇 Weissgerber 2015 PLOS Biology 的論文「Beyond Bar and Line Graphs: Time for a New Data Presentation Paradigm」。Yuki 讀到第三頁時整個人愣住：作者用同一組 mean ± SEM 構造出五種截然不同的 underlying distribution——常態、雙峰、嚴重偏態、有離群值、樣本量極小——而 bar chart 全部看起來「一模一樣」。

那晚 Yuki 用 ggplot2 把 12 張圖重畫成 boxplot + jitter，赫然發現自己的「顯著差異」其實有一組是被兩個離群值撐起來的。她調整統計檢定、補上 Welch's t-test 與 Cohen's d，再加 color-blind safe palette。三週後 paper 被 Cell Reports 接受，reviewer 特別讚許「figure transparency」。Yuki 後來把那篇 Weissgerber 論文釘在電腦旁。

PhD student Yuki prepares her first paper figures: twelve gorgeous bar charts with SEM error bars, bright red-vs-green contrasts everywhere. She hands the PDF to her PI Prof. Sato, brimming with pride. The next day in the meeting room, he says only: "No bar charts with SEM. Switch to boxplot + jitter — show every raw point."

Yuki bristles — isn't bar + SEM what everyone uses? Sato doesn't explain; he just hands her Weissgerber et al. 2015 in PLOS Biology, "Beyond Bar and Line Graphs: Time for a New Data Presentation Paradigm." By page three Yuki is stunned: the authors construct five radically different distributions — normal, bimodal, heavily skewed, with outliers, tiny n — that all produce identical-looking bar charts with identical mean ± SEM.

That night Yuki redraws all twelve figures as boxplot + jitter in ggplot2 and discovers her "significant difference" in panel 4 is driven by two outliers. She switches to Welch's t-test, adds Cohen's d with 95% CI, and applies a color-blind-safe viridis palette. Three weeks later the paper is accepted at Cell Reports; reviewers specifically praise "figure transparency." Yuki pins the Weissgerber paper next to her monitor.

14 個關鍵術語（雙版本）

Results section
結果章節
只說「我看到什麼」——不要說「這代表什麼」。像新聞主播報事實。
Just "what I saw" — never "what it means." Like a news anchor reporting facts.
IMRaD 結構的第三段；報告觀察到的數據、統計檢定結果、效應量、CI；不含解讀（解讀屬 Discussion）。
Third section of IMRaD; reports observed data, statistical tests, effect sizes, CIs; no interpretation (that belongs in Discussion).
Table 1
基線特徵表
研究的「演員名單」：每組的人數、年齡、男女比、基本健康指標。
The study's "cast list": each group's n, age, sex ratio, baseline health metrics.
列出 cohort 基線特徵（年齡、性別、共病、實驗室值）；只放 baseline，不放 outcome；propensity-matched 須附 SMD。
Lists baseline cohort characteristics (age, sex, comorbidities, labs); baseline only, not outcomes; propensity-matched cohorts need SMD.
p-value
p 值
「如果其實沒差，看到這種結果的機率有多小？」——只回答「有沒有差」，不說「差多少」。
"If there's really no difference, how unlikely is this result?" — answers "is there a difference" only, not "how much."
在 H₀ 為真假設下觀察到目前或更極端結果的條件機率（Fisher 1925）。ASA 2016 明確：p 不能單獨判斷重要性。
Conditional probability of observing current or more extreme data under H₀ (Fisher 1925). ASA 2016: p alone cannot judge importance.
95% CI
95% 信賴區間
「真值大概在這個範圍裡」——範圍越窄越精準。
"The true value is probably somewhere in this range" — narrower = more precise.
若重複抽樣無限次、其中 95% 區間會覆蓋母體真值。Neyman 1937；報告效應量必附 CI。
If we resampled infinitely, 95% of computed CIs would cover the true parameter (Neyman 1937). Always report CI with effect size.
Effect size
效應量
「差多少」——p 告訴你「有差」、effect size 告訴你「差到底有多大」。
"How big is the difference" — p says "yes/no," effect size says "how much."
標準化或原始的差異強度量；包含 Cohen's d、Hedges' g、OR、HR、r²、log2FC、AUC 等；ASA 2016 強烈建議與 p 配對。
Standardized or raw magnitude of difference: Cohen's d, Hedges' g, OR, HR, r², log2FC, AUC. ASA 2016 strongly urges pairing with p.
Cohen's d
Cohen's d
「兩堆數據離得多遠？」用合併標準差當尺。
"How far apart are two data piles?" — measured in pooled-SD units.
(M₁−M₂)/SD_pooled；Cohen 1988 提出 small=0.2 / medium=0.5 / large=0.8。小樣本應改用 Hedges' g。
(M₁−M₂)/SD_pooled; Cohen 1988 thresholds small=0.2 / medium=0.5 / large=0.8. Use Hedges' g for small n.
Odds Ratio (OR)
勝算比
「A 組得病的『賠率』是 B 組的幾倍？」OR=1 沒差；OR>1 風險高。
"A's odds of disease are how many times B's?" OR=1 no diff; OR>1 elevated risk.
case-control 與 logistic regression 標準效應量；OR = (a/b)/(c/d)。稀有事件下近似 RR；常見事件下會誇大。
Standard effect size in case-control and logistic regression; OR = (a/b)/(c/d). Approximates RR when events are rare; overstates it when common.
Hazard Ratio (HR)
風險比
「在每個時間點上，A 組『出事』的速度是 B 組的幾倍？」
"At any time point, A's instantaneous event rate is how many times B's?"
Cox proportional hazards model 的效應量（Cox 1972）；前提：hazards 在時間上成比例（用 Schoenfeld 殘差檢驗）。
Effect size from Cox proportional hazards regression (Cox 1972); requires proportionality of hazards (test via Schoenfeld residuals).
FDR (BH)
錯誤發現率
做 1 萬次比較時，「我說是的之中、有多少其實是錯的？」
When making 10,000 comparisons, "of those I called positive, how many are actually wrong?"
Benjamini-Hochberg 1995 JRSS-B；控制 expected FDR ≤ α。Omics 分析標配；比 Bonferroni 對 power 更友善。
Benjamini-Hochberg 1995 JRSS-B; controls expected FDR ≤ α. Standard in omics; less power-costly than Bonferroni.
Volcano plot
火山圖
差異基因的「火山」：X 軸是差距、Y 軸是信心；爆炸的點在山頂兩側。
The DEG "volcano": X = magnitude of difference, Y = confidence; explosive hits sit at the top corners.
log2 fold-change（x）vs −log10(adjusted p)（y）的散點圖；標出 FDR < 閾值且 |log2FC| 大的基因。Cui & Churchill 2003 推廣。
Scatter of log2FC (x) vs −log10(adjusted p) (y); highlight genes passing FDR threshold with large |log2FC|. Popularized by Cui & Churchill 2003.
Heatmap
熱圖
用顏色畫的表格：紅熱、藍冷；行列可重新排序找模式。
A color-painted table: red hot, blue cold; rows/cols re-ordered to expose patterns.
Eisen 1998 PNAS hierarchical clustering heatmap。注意 row scaling (z-score) vs raw、dendrogram、color scale (避免 jet)。
Eisen 1998 PNAS hierarchical clustering heatmap. Mind row scaling (z-score) vs raw, dendrograms, color scale (avoid jet).
Kaplan-Meier
存活曲線
「每一刻還活著的比例」隨時間下階梯。兩組曲線差距越大越顯著。
A step-down curve of "fraction still alive at each moment." Bigger gap between curves = bigger effect.
Kaplan-Meier 1958 JASA；考量 censoring；log-rank 檢定 + Cox HR + at-risk table 為標配。
Kaplan-Meier 1958 JASA; accounts for censoring; standard reporting = log-rank + Cox HR + at-risk table.
Viridis colormap
Viridis 色階
紫→綠→黃的色帶：印成黑白也看得出深淺，色盲也分得清。
A purple→green→yellow ramp: works in grayscale, robust to color blindness.
Smith & van der Walt 2015 Matplotlib 預設；perceptually uniform、color-blind safe；同家族 magma / inferno / plasma / cividis。
Smith & van der Walt 2015 (matplotlib default); perceptually uniform, color-blind safe. Family: magma / inferno / plasma / cividis.
Data-ink ratio
資料-墨水比
「圖上的墨水，有多少在畫真正的數據？」越高越好。
"Of all the ink on a chart, how much is actually data?" Higher = better.
Tufte 1983：data-ink / total-ink。「擦掉所有不傳達資料的墨水」——3D、漸層、重複網格都該砍。
Tufte 1983: data-ink / total-ink. "Erase non-data ink" — drop 3D, gradients, redundant grids.

完整流程

Σ 步驟：從原始數據到出版級圖

Σ₁
決定故事先寫下這張圖要回答哪 1 個問題——只 1 個。如果想塞 2 個，請拆成 2 panel 或 2 張圖。Write down the single question this figure answers — only one. If you want two, split into two panels or two figures.
Σ₂
選圖型 (decision tree)兩組比較 → boxplot+jitter；連續 vs 連續 → scatter+regression；存活 → Kaplan-Meier；高維 omics → volcano / heatmap / UMAP。Two-group → boxplot+jitter; continuous vs continuous → scatter+regression; survival → Kaplan-Meier; high-dim omics → volcano / heatmap / UMAP.
Σ₃
色盲安全色盤連續：viridis / magma / cividis；類別：ColorBrewer Set1/Set2 或 Okabe-Ito。永遠不要用 jet / rainbow。Continuous: viridis / magma / cividis. Categorical: ColorBrewer Set1/Set2 or Okabe-Ito. Never jet/rainbow.
Σ₄
寫 caption必含 n、所用統計檢定、誤差條代表 (SD / SEM / 95% CI)、校正方法。Caption 要能讓讀者不看正文也讀懂。Must include n, statistical test, what error bars represent (SD / SEM / 95% CI), correction method. Caption must stand alone.
Σ₅
算 effect size + 95% CI兩組均值用 Cohen's d 或 mean diff；類別 OR / RR；生存 HR；相關 r。永遠和 p 一起報告。Means → Cohen's d or mean diff; categorical → OR / RR; survival → HR; correlation → r. Always paired with p.
Σ₆
多重檢定加 FDR (BH)omics 或 ≥10 次比較需校正；用 Benjamini-Hochberg；report adjusted p 並標明方法。Omics or ≥10 comparisons require correction; use Benjamini-Hochberg; report adjusted p and state the method.
Σ₇
輸出 300 dpi PDF向量圖優先（PDF / SVG）；字體 ≥7 pt at print size；line width 0.5-1 pt；嵌字以避免遺失。Vector preferred (PDF / SVG); font ≥7 pt at print size; line width 0.5-1 pt; embed fonts to avoid loss.
Σ₈
交叉檢查 Methods每張 figure 對應的 cohort / 統計檢定 / 校正方法都要在 Methods 找得到；不要圖上寫了 Methods 沒寫。Every figure's cohort / test / correction must be findable in Methods; never let a figure use a method that isn't described.
專家辯論

三個爭論點：p、color、distribution

辯論 1
2026 年我們還需要 p 值嗎？
支持留下：p 值仍是「無差異假設下事件機率」的標準語言，FDA / EMA 監管仍以 p<0.05 為主軸。主張取消：Wasserstein 2019 TAS「Moving to a world beyond p<0.05」呼籲改報 effect size + CI；ASA 2016 明確 p 不能單獨判斷。實務折衷：p、effect size、CI 三者並陳——這也是本章主張。
Keep: p remains the standard language of "probability under no-difference"; FDA/EMA still anchor decisions on p<0.05. Abolish: Wasserstein 2019 TAS "Moving to a world beyond p<0.05" urges reporting effect size + CI instead; ASA 2016 says p alone is insufficient. Pragmatic middle: always report p, effect size, and CI together — the position this chapter takes.
辯論 2
color-blind safe 在 2026 是不是過度了？
支持：8% 男性紅綠色盲 + 期刊印刷常黑白 → viridis 永遠更安全。質疑：類別 ≥6 時 viridis 區辨不夠，Okabe-Ito 反而更清楚；過度執著 viridis 也是 chartjunk。實務建議：連續變項預設 viridis；類別 >6 改 Okabe-Ito 或自訂安全色盤。
Pro: 8% of men are red-green color blind; journals still print in grayscale — viridis is always safer. Con: With ≥6 categories viridis bands blur; Okabe-Ito is clearer; obsessing over viridis becomes chartjunk too. Practical: default viridis for continuous; switch to Okabe-Ito or custom safe palettes for >6 categories.
辯論 3
期刊是否該強制要求顯示分布（禁用 bar + SEM）？
支持強制：Weissgerber 2015 示範 bar + SEM 隱藏巨大分布差異；eLife、PLOS、Cell Reports 已在投稿指南推薦 boxplot+jitter。反對強制：n=3-5 小樣本反而 boxplot 不適合，bar 也能配合 individual dot；硬性禁止會誤傷正常用法。實務：n≥10 強制顯示分布；n<10 用 dot plot + 個體點。
Pro mandate: Weissgerber 2015 shows bar + SEM hides huge distribution differences; eLife, PLOS, Cell Reports already recommend boxplot+jitter. Against mandate: n=3-5 makes boxplot unfit; bar + individual dots can also work; a blanket ban harms valid use cases. Practical: n≥10 → mandatory distribution; n<10 → dot plot with individual points.
案例聚焦

兩個案例：說服與誤導

🌹
SUCCESS Nightingale 1858：玫瑰圖改革軍醫院

克里米亞戰爭中，Florence Nightingale 發現英軍士兵死於戰場以外（傷口感染、霍亂）的人數遠高於戰死。她用 polar area diagram 把每月死因比例「染色」呈現給維多利亞女王與英國國會：藍 = 可預防的疾病、紅 = 戰場傷、黑 = 其他。對比強烈、一眼即懂——國會通過軍醫院衛生改革法案，死亡率在兩年內從 42% 降到 2%。一張圖 = 兩年改革 = 十萬條人命。

During the Crimean War, Florence Nightingale found British soldiers dying off the battlefield (wound infection, cholera) at far higher rates than in combat. Her polar area diagram painted monthly causes of death for Queen Victoria and Parliament: blue = preventable disease, red = battle wounds, black = other. The contrast was instant — Parliament passed sanitation-reform legislation; mortality fell from 42% to 2% in two years. One figure → two years of reform → ~100,000 lives.

⚠️
WARNING IBM Watson Oncology 2018：華麗 AI 圖表掩蓋不安全建議

2018 年 STAT News 揭露 MD Anderson 內部文件：IBM Watson for Oncology 在 demo 上以鮮豔的「90%+ 準確率」與「比對 NCCN 指南」圖表向醫院推銷，但實際對某些病例會建議「對活動性出血的病人使用嚴重出血副作用的藥物」。出色的 figure 設計（光滑漸層、漂亮 confidence bar）讓決策者錯把「demo 視覺說服力」當成「臨床安全性」。教訓：圖表越華麗，越要追問「underlying data 是什麼？n 多少？n 是『病人』還是『demo 案例』？」

In 2018 STAT News uncovered MD Anderson internal docs: IBM Watson for Oncology demos used glossy "90%+ accuracy" charts and "matches NCCN guidelines" bars to win hospitals, while actually recommending — in certain cases — drugs with severe bleeding side effects for patients with active bleeding. Polished figure design (smooth gradients, beautiful confidence bars) led decision-makers to mistake demo polish for clinical safety. Lesson: the slicker the figure, the harder you must ask "what's the underlying data? what's n? is n 'patients' or 'demo cases'?"

陷阱

6 個典型陷阱（避開即贏）

① bar chart + SEM

隱藏分布、樣本量、離群值。同樣 mean ± SEM 可以對應 5 種完全不同的 distribution（Weissgerber 2015）。改用 boxplot + jitter 或 violin + dot。

Hides distribution, n, outliers. Identical mean ± SEM can match five very different distributions (Weissgerber 2015). Use boxplot + jitter or violin + dot.

② 只有 p、沒有 effect size

「p<0.05」不告訴你差多少。ASA 2016 強烈反對單獨報 p。每個 p 必須伴隨 mean diff / OR / HR / d 與 95% CI。

"p<0.05" never says how much. ASA 2016 strongly opposes p-alone reporting. Every p must come with mean diff / OR / HR / d and 95% CI.

③ rainbow / jet 色階

非 perceptually uniform、色盲不友善、印成灰階全毀。連續用 viridis 家族；類別用 ColorBrewer / Okabe-Ito。

Not perceptually uniform, color-blind hostile, ruined in grayscale. Continuous → viridis family; categorical → ColorBrewer / Okabe-Ito.

④ 故意切 y 軸

把 y 軸從 80% 切到 100% 讓 0.5% 差異看起來很大。Tufte 稱為「lie factor」。除非另有合理理由，連續 y 軸應從 0 開始。

Truncating y from 80–100% makes a 0.5% difference look huge — Tufte's "lie factor." Unless justified, continuous y should start at 0.

⑤ 3D pie chart

3D 透視扭曲面積、難比較類別。Few 2010「Save the Pies for Dessert」——pie chart 在 ≥4 類已不可讀，3D 更糟。改用 bar chart 或 stacked-bar。

3D perspective distorts area, makes categories hard to compare. Few 2010 "Save the Pies for Dessert" — pies fail at ≥4 categories; 3D is worse. Use bar or stacked-bar.

⑥ caption 沒 n

沒寫 n=? 讀者不知道誤差條意義；biological vs technical replicates 不分。Caption 必含 n、所用 test、誤差條代表、校正方法。

Without n, readers can't interpret error bars; biological vs technical replicates blur. Captions must list n, test, error-bar meaning, correction method.

範例對照

一句結果：壞 vs 好

✗ 不完整

6 個反思題

① 為什麼 bar + SEM 會誤導？同一組 mean ± SEM 能掩蓋哪些分布形態？

② 何時用 median (IQR)、何時用 mean (SD)？資料偏態與離群值怎麼判斷？
② When to use median (IQR) vs mean (SD)? How do you judge skewness and outliers?
③ 用你自己的話解釋什麼是 95% CI？它不是什麼？（小心常見誤解）
③ Explain 95% CI in your own words. What is it NOT? (Beware common misconceptions.)
④ 為什麼 omics 必須做 FDR 校正？只用 Bonferroni 有何代價？
④ Why must omics use FDR correction? What's the cost of using Bonferroni instead?
⑤ 你的圖能否在黑白列印與紅綠色盲眼中皆清楚？拿這兩個鏡頭檢查最近一張圖。
⑤ Is your figure readable in grayscale AND for red-green color blindness? Test your most recent figure with both lenses.
⑥ 你 caption 寫了 n、所用 test、誤差條代表、校正方法嗎？少哪一項？
⑥ Does your caption state n, statistical test, error-bar meaning, correction method? Which is missing?

進階 a
SuperPlots：實驗 n vs 細胞 n 的區分 (Lord 2020 JCB)

Lord et al. 2020 J Cell Biol（doi:10.1083/jcb.202001064）提出 SuperPlots：在 boxplot/violin 之上疊加「實驗平均點」與「細胞個體點」，雙層 jitter 讓讀者一眼分辨 biological replicate (n=實驗) vs technical replicate (n=細胞)。避免將「每張影像 200 個細胞」當成「n=200」的常見誤用。

Lord et al. 2020 J Cell Biol (doi:10.1083/jcb.202001064) propose SuperPlots: overlay "experiment-mean points" and "individual cell points" on boxplot/violin with dual jitter, making biological-n (experiments) vs technical-n (cells) immediately visible. Prevents the classic abuse of treating "200 cells per image" as "n=200."

進階 b
UpSet plot 取代 Venn (Lex 2014 IEEE TVCG)

Lex et al. 2014 IEEE TVCG（doi:10.1109/TVCG.2014.2346248）UpSet plot：當集合數 ≥4 時 Venn 已不可讀，UpSet 用矩陣（行=集合、列=交集組合）+ 長條（交集大小）視覺化任意數集合的交集結構。R 套件 UpSetR / ComplexHeatmap、Python upsetplot 皆可用。

Lex et al. 2014 IEEE TVCG (doi:10.1109/TVCG.2014.2346248) UpSet plot: Venn diagrams break down at ≥4 sets. UpSet visualizes arbitrary-size set intersections with a matrix (rows=sets, columns=intersection combinations) + bar chart (intersection sizes). R: UpSetR / ComplexHeatmap; Python: upsetplot.

進階 c
2024-2026 互動圖時代：Plotly / Observable / Quarto

主流期刊 supplementary 接受互動圖：Plotly（hover tooltip、zoom）、Observable Plot（D3 grammar、流暢動態）、Quarto-revealjs（reveal.js + 程式碼 chunk + 互動圖一體成形）。Cell / Nature 2024-2025 已多篇 supplementary 為 interactive HTML；reviewer 也開始要求「請提供互動版本驗證 cluster 邊界」。但主圖仍須有 publication-quality static PDF 作為紀錄版本。

Mainstream journal supplementaries now accept interactive figures: Plotly (hover, zoom), Observable Plot (D3 grammar, smooth motion), Quarto-revealjs (reveal.js + code chunks + interactive figures in one document). Cell / Nature 2024-25 supplementaries are increasingly interactive HTML; reviewers ask for "interactive version to verify cluster boundaries." Main figures still need a publication-quality static PDF as the canonical version.

軼事：Tufte 自費出版的傳奇

Edward Tufte 1983 年寫完《The Visual Display of Quantitative Information》後，被每一家主流出版社拒絕——「資料視覺化是小眾領域」。Tufte 不死心：他抵押自己的房子、自學排版印刷、創立 Graphics Press 自費出版。書一出版立刻成為現象級暢銷書，至今銷量超過 150 萬冊，被翻譯成多國語言。他現在每本書仍由 Graphics Press 親自出版，掌控每一頁的版式與紙質。一句話：把資料視覺化當成藝術的人，最後改變了科學界整個資料呈現的標準。

When Edward Tufte finished "The Visual Display of Quantitative Information" in 1983, every mainstream publisher rejected it — "data visualization is too niche." Tufte refused to give up: he mortgaged his house, taught himself typography and printing, founded Graphics Press, and self-published. The book became an instant phenomenon — over 1.5 million copies sold, translated into many languages. He still publishes every book through Graphics Press, controlling every page layout and paper choice. One line: the person who treated data visualization as art ended up changing how all of science reports its data.

概覽

Results 的黃金鐵則

鐵則 1：只報告，不解讀。解讀放 Discussion。
鐵則 2：圖能獨立說故事。讀者不看正文，只看圖與 caption 也能懂主結論。
鐵則 3：所有 p 值都要有效應量 + 95% CI 配對。p 值只說「有沒有差」，效應量說「差多少」。

Rule 1: Report, don't interpret. Interpretation belongs in Discussion.
Rule 2: Figures must stand alone. A reader skimming only figures + captions should grasp the main findings.
Rule 3: Every p value needs an effect size + 95% CI. p tells you "is there a difference"; effect size tells you "how much."

💡
結構提示：Results 段落順序通常是 (1) Cohort/sample 描述 (Table 1) → (2) Primary outcome (Figure 1-2) → (3) Secondary outcomes (Figure 3-4) → (4) Sensitivity / subgroup analyses。每個 figure 對應 1 段文字。 Structure hint: Results paragraphs typically flow (1) Cohort/sample description (Table 1) → (2) Primary outcome (Figs 1-2) → (3) Secondary outcomes (Figs 3-4) → (4) Sensitivity/subgroup analyses. One paragraph per figure.

圖表設計

三、Figure 設計 7 大原則

① One figure, one message

每張主圖傳達一個核心訊息，多 panel (A/B/C/D) 是同一個故事的多個面向，不是塞滿空間。

One figure conveys one core message. Multi-panel (A/B/C/D) figures are facets of one story, not space-fillers.

② 色盲友善

避開 jet/rainbow。連續變項用 viridis / magma；類別變項用 ColorBrewer Set1/Set2。約 8% 男性、0.5% 女性是紅綠色盲。

Avoid jet / rainbow. Continuous: viridis / magma. Categorical: ColorBrewer Set1/Set2. ~8% of men and 0.5% of women have red-green color blindness.

③ 字體 ≥7 pt

大多期刊要求印刷後最小字體 ≥7 pt (約 2 mm)。300 dpi、export 時記得算實際尺寸。

Most journals require ≥7 pt (~2 mm) at print size. Export at 300 dpi and verify physical dimensions.

④ 去除 chartjunk

3D 效果、漸層背景、雙 Y 軸都要小心。Edward Tufte 的 data-ink ratio：墨水越多用在 data 越好。

Drop 3D, gradient backgrounds, dual Y-axes (with caution). Tufte's data-ink ratio: maximize ink dedicated to data.

⑤ 顯示原始數據

單純 bar chart 隱藏分布。改用 boxplot + jitter / violin / dot plot 讓讀者看到 n 與離群值。

Bar charts hide distributions. Use boxplot + jitter / violin / dot plot so readers see n and outliers.

⑥ Caption 完整自包含

Caption 應寫：n 數、所用統計檢定、誤差條代表的是 SD/SEM/95% CI、什麼校正法。

Captions must state: n, statistical test used, what error bars represent (SD/SEM/95% CI), correction method.

範例對照

四、結果段落寫作對照

❌ 解讀混入

「治療組的 PFS 顯著比對照組好 (p=0.001)，這顯示我們的新療法非常有效，可能改變臨床實踐。」
(「非常有效」「可能改變臨床實踐」是 Discussion，不是 Results。也缺 HR 與 95% CI。)

"Treated PFS was significantly better than control (p=0.001), showing our therapy is highly effective and may change clinical practice."
("highly effective" / "may change practice" belong in Discussion. HR and 95% CI also missing.)

✅ 只報告

「治療組的中位 PFS 為 11.4 個月 (95% CI 9.8–13.2)，對照組為 7.6 個月 (95% CI 6.4–9.1)。Cox 模型 HR 為 0.62 (95% CI 0.48–0.81; log-rank p=0.001)，調整年齡、性別與 PD-L1 後 HR 維持 0.65 (95% CI 0.50–0.84) (Figure 2)。」

"Median PFS was 11.4 months (95% CI 9.8–13.2) in the treated group vs 7.6 months (95% CI 6.4–9.1) in control. Cox model HR 0.62 (95% CI 0.48–0.81; log-rank p=0.001); adjusted HR 0.65 (95% CI 0.50–0.84) after age, sex, and PD-L1 (Figure 2)."

❌ 只報 p 值

「Gene X 在腫瘤組顯著上調 (p=0.03)。」

"Gene X was significantly upregulated in tumors (p=0.03)."

✅ 完整版

「Gene X 在腫瘤 (n=24) 表達中位數 4.2 TPM (IQR 3.1–5.6)，正常組織 (n=18) 為 1.9 TPM (IQR 1.2–2.7)；Mann-Whitney U=89, p=0.03，BH 校正後 q=0.08，效應量 r=0.34 (Figure 3A)。」

"Median Gene X expression was 4.2 TPM (IQR 3.1–5.6) in tumors (n=24) vs 1.9 TPM (IQR 1.2–2.7) in normal (n=18). Mann-Whitney U=89, p=0.03, BH-adjusted q=0.08, effect size r=0.34 (Figure 3A)."

決策引導

五、選對圖型決策樹

🌳 什麼數據用什麼圖？

Q1:
連續變項分佈？→ Histogram / density / boxplot + jitter / violin。
Q2:
兩個連續變項關係？→ Scatter + 回歸線 + 95% CI band。n 大時用 hexbin。
Q3:
類別 vs 連續？→ Boxplot + dot overlay (避免純 bar chart)。
Q4:
時間序列 / 存活？→ Kaplan-Meier 含 risk table + log-rank p。
Q5:
多基因 vs 多樣本？→ Heatmap (z-score 標準化、annotate columns、用 viridis)。
Q6:
多重比較顯著性？→ Volcano (x: log2FC, y: -log10 q) 或 Manhattan (GWAS)。
Q7:
分類器效能？→ ROC + AUC + 95% CI；不要只報 accuracy。
Q1:
Continuous distribution? → Histogram / density / boxplot + jitter / violin.
Q2:
Two continuous variables? → Scatter + regression line + 95% CI band; hexbin for large n.
Q3:
Categorical vs continuous? → Boxplot + dot overlay (avoid plain bar charts).
Q4:
Time series / survival? → Kaplan-Meier with risk table + log-rank p.
Q5:
Many genes × samples? → Heatmap (z-score, column annotations, viridis).
Q6:
Many comparisons w/ significance? → Volcano (log2FC vs -log10 q) or Manhattan (GWAS).
Q7:
Classifier performance? → ROC + AUC + 95% CI; never only accuracy.

library(ggplot2); library(viridis)
p <- ggplot(df, aes(group, expr, fill=group)) +
  geom_boxplot(outlier.shape=NA, alpha=0.7) +
  geom_jitter(width=0.2, size=1.2, alpha=0.6) +
  scale_fill_viridis_d() +
  labs(x="", y="Expression (TPM)") +
  theme_classic(base_size=8) +
  theme(legend.position="none",
        axis.text=element_text(color="black"))
ggsave("fig2a.pdf", p, width=3.5, height=3, units="in", dpi=300)
import matplotlib.pyplot as plt
import seaborn as sns

sns.boxplot(data=df, x="group", y="expr", palette="viridis",
