故事開場

場景：Alex 與 reviewer 的「我無法重現你的 pipeline」

SCENE · 2025 · scRNA-seq paper

從 desk-reject 到 Zenodo DOI——一個博士生的覺醒

博士生 Alex 投出了人生第一篇 scRNA-seq 論文，題目是「TNBC 化療後免疫細胞態變化」。他自認 Methods 寫得鉅細靡遺：「使用 Seurat 進行 QC、PCA、UMAP、Louvain clustering 與 differential expression。差異基因用 ggplot2 可視化。」三週後 reviewer 2 回信只有兩句：「We cannot reproduce your pipeline. Which Seurat version? What resolution? Where is the code?」

Alex 當下覺得委屈——「明明該寫的都寫了」。但實驗室師姐 Dora 翻出她去年被 Nature Communications 接受的 paper，逐段對照：版本號（Seurat v5.1.0, R 4.4.1）、隨機種子（set.seed(42)）、cluster resolution=0.6、PCs=30、UMAP n.neighbors=30。每一行都精確到「下個人不必猜」。

Alex 花了一整週重做：跑 sessionInfo() 把 R 套件版本鎖到 supplementary、把分析腳本推到 GitHub、領了 Zenodo DOI、用 EQUATOR 的 MIBBI/STROBE 自我核對。為了 ctDNA 子實驗，他追加了 IRB 編號與 informed consent 條款。最後在 Methods 末尾加上一段 Code & Data Availability。

修改稿三個月後接受，bioRxiv 同步上預印本。Reviewer 2 在公開的 review 中寫：「The revised Methods are exemplary — every step is now reproducible.」Alex 把這封 review 截圖貼在筆電上，成為他往後每次寫 Methods 的提醒：「方法不是給自己看的，是寫給 5 年後讀者重做用的。」

PhD student Alex submitted his first scRNA-seq paper — "Immune cell-state shifts in TNBC after chemotherapy." He believed his Methods were thorough: "Seurat was used for QC, PCA, UMAP, Louvain clustering, and differential expression; ggplot2 for visualization." Three weeks later reviewer 2 replied in two sentences: "We cannot reproduce your pipeline. Which Seurat version? What resolution? Where is the code?"

Alex felt wronged — "I wrote everything that mattered." But senior labmate Dora pulled out her own Nat Commun paper and went paragraph by paragraph: package versions (Seurat v5.1.0, R 4.4.1), random seed (set.seed(42)), cluster resolution=0.6, PCs=30, UMAP n.neighbors=30. Every line was precise enough that "the next person doesn't have to guess."

Alex spent a week redoing it: ran sessionInfo() and froze all R package versions to a supplementary table, pushed the analysis script to GitHub, minted a Zenodo DOI, and self-checked against EQUATOR's STROBE checklist. For a ctDNA sub-experiment, he added the IRB number and informed-consent statement. A formal Code & Data Availability paragraph closed the Methods section.

Three months after resubmission, the paper was accepted, with the manuscript also released on bioRxiv. Reviewer 2's public comment: "The revised Methods are exemplary — every step is now reproducible." Alex screenshotted that line and pinned it to his laptop. His rule for every Methods section since: "Methods aren't for me — they're for the reader who has to redo it 5 years from now."

核心詞彙

14 個關鍵術語（雙版本）

每個詞兩個版本：🧒「給小孩看」用比喻、📐「給研究者看」給定義。

Each term has two versions: 🧒 "for kids" uses a metaphor; 📐 "for researchers" gives the formal definition.

Methods section
方法章節
論文的「食譜」——告訴別人怎麼把你的菜煮一次。
The paper's "recipe" — tells others how to cook your dish.
IMRaD 結構中 M 部分；應包含 study design、participants、variables、sample size、analysis、ethics 等 ≥5 子段，目標：可被獨立重現。
The M in IMRaD; must contain ≥5 subsections — study design, participants, variables, sample size, analysis, ethics. Target: independent reproducibility.
EQUATOR Network
EQUATOR 網絡
「報告寫作的食譜總集」——每種研究都有對應的清單告訴你該寫什麼。
"A cookbook of cookbooks" — one checklist per study type telling you what to report.
Enhancing the QUAlity and Transparency Of health Research；2008 成立、Oxford 託管；目前維護 500+ reporting guidelines。
Enhancing the QUAlity and Transparency Of health Research; founded 2008, hosted at Oxford; maintains 500+ reporting guidelines.
STROBE
STROBE 觀察性研究清單
如果你的研究是「在旁邊看」而不是「主動做」，就用這份清單。
If your study "watches" rather than "intervenes," use this checklist.
Strengthening the Reporting of Observational Studies in Epidemiology；von Elm 2007 PLoS Med；cohort / case-control / cross-sectional 共 22 項。
Strengthening the Reporting of Observational Studies in Epidemiology; von Elm 2007 PLoS Med; 22 items across cohort / case-control / cross-sectional.
CONSORT
CONSORT 臨床試驗清單
如果你做的是「給藥 vs 給安慰劑」這種隨機分派試驗，用這份。
If you randomly assigned people to drug vs placebo, this checklist is for you.
CONsolidated Standards Of Reporting Trials；Schulz 2010 BMJ；CONSORT 2025 強化 estimand、harms、open data。
CONsolidated Standards Of Reporting Trials; Schulz 2010 BMJ; CONSORT 2025 strengthens estimand, harms, open data.
ARRIVE
ARRIVE 動物實驗清單
老鼠、斑馬魚的實驗該寫的事——年齡、性別、住哪、吃什麼，全要交代。
For mouse / zebrafish studies — age, sex, housing, diet must all be reported.
Animal Research: Reporting of In Vivo Experiments；Percie du Sert 2020 PLoS Biol；ARRIVE 2.0 含 essential 10 + recommended set。
Animal Research: Reporting of In Vivo Experiments; Percie du Sert 2020 PLoS Biol; ARRIVE 2.0 has essential 10 + recommended set.
TRIPOD+AI
TRIPOD+AI 機器學習模型清單
用 AI 蓋了一個「預測病人會不會生病」的模型？這份清單告訴你怎麼寫。
Built an AI model to predict disease? This checklist tells you how to report it.
Collins 2024 BMJ doi:10.1136/bmj-2023-078378；27 主項 + AI-specific items：資料來源、特徵工程、外部驗證、calibration。
Collins 2024 BMJ doi:10.1136/bmj-2023-078378; 27 main items + AI-specific items: data sources, features, external validation, calibration.
PRISMA
PRISMA 系統性回顧清單
如果你的論文是「整理 200 篇別人的論文」——這份是你的清單。
If your paper synthesizes 200 other papers, this is your checklist.
Preferred Reporting Items for Systematic reviews and Meta-Analyses；Page 2021 BMJ；PRISMA 2020 含 flow diagram + 27 items。
Preferred Reporting Items for Systematic reviews and Meta-Analyses; Page 2021 BMJ; PRISMA 2020 with flow diagram + 27 items.
Reproducibility
可重現性
「同樣食材、同樣食譜，做出來味道一樣」——這才算科學。
"Same ingredients, same recipe, same taste" — that's what counts as science.
NASEM 2019 三層級：computational reproducibility (相同 code 跑出相同結果)、replicability (新資料相同結論)、generalizability。
NASEM 2019 three levels: computational reproducibility (same code → same result), replicability (new data → same conclusion), generalizability.
Code Availability
程式碼公開聲明
「我的食譜放在這個網址，你可以照著做」——指引讀者去哪拿 code。
"My recipe is at this URL, follow along" — tells readers where the code is.
標準格式：GitHub URL + commit hash + license + Zenodo DOI snapshot；2026 Cell / Nature Press 期刊已強制。
Standard format: GitHub URL + commit hash + license + Zenodo DOI snapshot; mandated by Cell / Nature Press in 2026.
Data Availability
資料可取得聲明
「實驗數據放在哪？要怎麼拿？」每篇論文都要回答這題。
"Where's the raw data and how do I get it?" Every paper must answer this.
優先順序：公開 repository (GEO/SRA/Zenodo/Figshare + DOI) > controlled access (dbGaP/EGA) > "upon reasonable request"（最弱、漸被禁）。
Priority: public repo (GEO/SRA/Zenodo/Figshare + DOI) > controlled access (dbGaP/EGA) > "upon reasonable request" (weakest, increasingly disallowed).
Sample-size justification
樣本量正當性
「為什麼要找 50 個人不是 5 個？」事先計算才不會被退稿。
"Why 50 people not 5?" Pre-calculate it so reviewers don't reject the paper.
需報 α、β (power=1-β)、預期 effect size、dropout、最終 n；常用工具：G*Power (Faul 2007)、PASS、R pwr。
Report α, β (power=1-β), expected effect size, dropout, and final n; common tools: G*Power (Faul 2007), PASS, R pwr.
IRB / IACUC
人體 / 動物倫理委員會
「做人體 / 動物實驗要先得到許可」——沒這個審查號就不該寫論文。
"You need permission before studying humans / animals" — no approval number, no paper.
IRB = Institutional Review Board (人體)；IACUC = Institutional Animal Care and Use Committee (動物)；必須寫核准號 + Declaration of Helsinki (2024) / ARRIVE 2.0 引用。
IRB = Institutional Review Board (human); IACUC = Institutional Animal Care and Use Committee (animal); must cite approval number + Declaration of Helsinki (2024) / ARRIVE 2.0.
Random seed
隨機種子
「亂數骰子的起始位置」——同一個種子，下次擲出來一模一樣。
"The dice's starting position" — same seed, same throw next time.
隨機過程 (UMAP, t-SNE, clustering, train-test split, ML) 必須 set.seed(42) 或 np.random.seed(42)；缺乏 = 結果不可重現。
Any stochastic step (UMAP, t-SNE, clustering, train-test split, ML) needs set.seed(42) or np.random.seed(42); without it = non-reproducible.
FAIR principles
FAIR 原則
資料要 4 件事：找得到 (F)、拿得到 (A)、能跟別資料合 (I)、可再用 (R)。
Data must be 4 things: Findable, Accessible, Interoperable, Reusable.
Wilkinson 2016 Sci Data；NIH Data Management & Sharing Policy 2023 全面採用；DOI + 標準化 metadata + API + 開放授權。
Wilkinson 2016 Sci Data; adopted by NIH Data Management & Sharing Policy 2023; DOI + standardized metadata + API + open license.

比喻

Methods 就是一份食譜

🍳

想像你拿到一份食譜，上面只寫「加一點鹽、煮到熟、想吃辣可以加點辣」——你能煮出跟原作者一樣的菜嗎？絕對不行。同樣的，Methods 寫「我們用 Seurat 跑了 clustering」也是徒勞——版本？resolution？PCs 數？random seed？都是「加一點鹽」級的模糊。

好的食譜會說「5g NaCl，中火 8 分鐘，油溫 180°C」。好的 Methods 會說「Seurat v5.1.0 (R 4.4.1)，resolution=0.6，top 30 PCs，random seed=42」。差別不在於「夠不夠詳細」，而在於「下一個人能不能跟你做出一模一樣的菜」。

Imagine getting a recipe that says "add some salt, cook till done, add chili if you like spicy." Could you reproduce the original chef's dish? Of course not. The same is true for "We used Seurat for clustering" — version? resolution? PCs? random seed? They're all "some salt"-level vague.

A good recipe says "5g NaCl, medium heat 8 min, oil at 180°C." Good Methods say "Seurat v5.1.0 (R 4.4.1), resolution=0.6, top 30 PCs, random seed=42." The difference isn't "how detailed" — it's "can the next person cook the same dish."

步驟導引

從零開始寫 Methods 的 8 個步驟

Σ 可重現 Methods 的工作流
Σ₁
挑選 EQUATOR checklist。到 equator-network.org 找對應研究類型：RCT → CONSORT 2025、cohort/case-control → STROBE、SR → PRISMA 2020、動物 → ARRIVE 2.0、ML 模型 → TRIPOD+AI。下載 PDF 並在 Word/Quarto 旁邊開著。
Pick the EQUATOR checklist. At equator-network.org find the one for your study type: RCT → CONSORT 2025, cohort/case-control → STROBE, SR → PRISMA 2020, animal → ARRIVE 2.0, ML model → TRIPOD+AI. Download the PDF and keep it open beside Word/Quarto.
Σ₂
鎖死 study design 一句話。例如：「This is a single-center retrospective cohort of stage III-IV TNBC patients (NTUH, 2020-2024)」。寫死設計類型、地點、時間、單/多中心。
Lock the study-design sentence. Example: "This is a single-center retrospective cohort of stage III-IV TNBC patients (NTUH, 2020-2024)." Pin design, site, period, single/multi-center.
Σ₃
定義 inclusion / exclusion。每條規則一行：年齡範圍、診斷準則、用藥史、樣本品質門檻。Omics：物種、組織、batch、accession。「不寫的就是不知道」是審查者預設。
Define inclusion / exclusion. One line per rule: age range, diagnostic criteria, medication history, sample-quality thresholds. Omics: species, tissue, batch, accession. Reviewers assume "what you didn't write, you didn't think about."
Σ₄
樣本量正當性（用 G*Power / pwr）。列出 α、β、預期 effect size、預期 dropout、最終 n。RCT、動物、ML 模型尤其必要；探索性 omics 也應給「power for primary contrast」。
Sample-size justification (G*Power / pwr). List α, β, expected effect size, expected dropout, final n. Mandatory for RCT, animal, ML; even exploratory omics should give "power for the primary contrast."
Σ₅
軟體版本逐一列。R 跑 sessionInfo()、Python 跑 pip freeze > requirements.txt；Cellranger、bcftools、Seurat、Scanpy、scikit-learn、torch、CUDA、OS 全部列入 Supplementary Table。「Seurat」沒版本 = Methods 失敗。
List every software version. Run sessionInfo() in R, pip freeze > requirements.txt in Python; Cellranger, bcftools, Seurat, Scanpy, scikit-learn, torch, CUDA, OS all go into a Supplementary Table. "Seurat" without a version = Methods failure.
Σ₆
預先註冊 (pre-register)。RCT → ClinicalTrials.gov；SR → PROSPERO；其他 → OSF Registry / AsPredicted。先註冊再做，可避免 HARKing 被指控。Registered Report 是更強形式。
Pre-register. RCT → ClinicalTrials.gov; SR → PROSPERO; other → OSF Registry / AsPredicted. Pre-register before running prevents HARKing accusations. Registered Reports go further.
Σ₇
Deposit code 與 data。Code → GitHub repo + tag/release，再到 Zenodo 領 DOI snapshot；Data → 原始 fastq/bam 到 GEO/SRA；處理後物件到 Zenodo/Figshare；clinical → dbGaP/EGA (controlled)。每樣留下 accession/DOI。
Deposit code and data. Code → GitHub repo + tag/release, then mint a Zenodo DOI snapshot; Data → raw fastq/bam to GEO/SRA; processed objects to Zenodo/Figshare; clinical → dbGaP/EGA (controlled). Keep every accession/DOI.
Σ₈
寫 Code & Data Availability + Ethics 段。明確點出 GitHub URL + commit hash + license + Zenodo DOI + 資料 accession + IRB/IACUC 編號 + Declaration of Helsinki (2024) 引用 + AI 工具使用揭露（如有）。這段是審查者第一個看的。
Write the Code & Data Availability + Ethics paragraphs. Spell out GitHub URL + commit hash + license + Zenodo DOI + data accession + IRB/IACUC number + Declaration of Helsinki (2024) citation + AI-tool disclosure (if applicable). Reviewers read this first.
專家辯論

3 個 Methods 寫作的灰色地帶

爭論 1
Methods 該放正文還是 Supplementary？
正方（Nature/Science 風格）：主文限 1500-3000 字，所以詳細 protocol 應移到 Online Methods / Supplementary，讓主文聚焦故事敘事。
反方（PLOS/eLife 風格）：Methods 是論文的「不過時部分」，藏在 Supplementary 等於削弱可重現性；應留在主文。
共識：high-level 概覽留主文，detailed protocol（試劑、儀器設定、程式參數）入 Supplementary 並加 DOI。讀者一查就找得到。
For (Nature/Science style): Main text caps at 1500-3000 words, so detailed protocols go to Online Methods / Supplementary; main text focuses on narrative.
Against (PLOS/eLife style): Methods is the "timeless" part — hiding it in Supplementary weakens reproducibility; keep in main text.
Consensus: High-level overview in main text; detailed protocol (reagents, instrument settings, code parameters) in Supplementary with a DOI. Readers can locate it.
爭論 2
AI 寫的程式碼，要不要在 Methods 揭露？
正方（保守派）：AI 是工具，跟 IDE 自動補全一樣，不必每段都揭露；只要結果可重現即可。
反方（透明派）：Cell Press 2026 已明令揭露 AI 工具的 model name、version、prompt、role；不揭露 = 違反 publishing ethics。
當前實務：若 AI 僅做 boilerplate（函式樣板、命名建議），可在 Acknowledgments 一句帶過；若 AI 設計演算法/分析邏輯，必須在 Methods 詳列。
For (conservative): AI is a tool like IDE autocomplete — no need to disclose every snippet if results reproduce.
Against (transparency): Cell Press 2026 mandates disclosing AI-tool model name, version, prompt, role; non-disclosure = ethics breach.
Current practice: Boilerplate (function templates, naming hints) → one line in Acknowledgments; AI designing algorithms / analysis logic → full Methods detail.
爭論 3
「data available upon reasonable request」還能用嗎？
研究 (Gabelica 2022 J Clin Epidemiol)：聲稱「upon request」的 1792 篇論文中，僅 6.7% 真的能拿到資料——這句話是 reproducibility 的最大謊言。
限制條件：當資料含個資（病人資訊、罕見疾病）受法規約束，"controlled access via dbGaP/EGA" 才是正解，仍須給 accession。
結論：純探索性 omics、模擬資料、code — 都應公開 deposit；唯有個資 / 智財 / 商業敏感才能「controlled access」，但不可空話 "upon request"。
Evidence (Gabelica 2022 J Clin Epidemiol): Of 1792 papers saying "upon request," only 6.7% actually shared data — biggest reproducibility lie.
Constraints: When data contains PII (patient info, rare disease) and is regulated, "controlled access via dbGaP/EGA" is the answer — but still give an accession.
Bottom line: Exploratory omics / simulated data / code → public deposit. Only PII / IP / commercially sensitive → controlled access, with accession; never empty "upon request."
案例聚光

一好一壞：方法寫作的兩極案例

⭐
FAMOUS SUCCESS — ENCODE Project 2012 Nature：ENCODE 集合 32 篇論文同時刊出，1640 個資料集全部開放下載；每篇 Methods 在 Online 補充近 100 頁 protocols、code、metadata schema、quality control thresholds。GitHub & UCSC Genome Browser snapshots 完整公開。從那時起，「ENCODE-style Methods」成為大型 consortium 論文的標竿，且因可重現性極高，至 2026 年仍被頻繁引用、再用、擴充——這就是「Methods 是論文唯一不過時的部分」最好的證據。
FAMOUS SUCCESS — ENCODE Project 2012 Nature: ENCODE released 32 papers simultaneously, with all 1640 datasets publicly downloadable; each paper's Methods provided ~100 pages of online supplementary protocols, code, metadata schema, and QC thresholds. GitHub & UCSC Genome Browser snapshots were fully open. Since then "ENCODE-style Methods" has been the benchmark for large-consortium papers — and because reproducibility is exceptional, they are still being cited, reused, and extended in 2026. The best proof that "Methods are the only timeless part of a paper."
✨ 成功
⚠️
FAMOUS FAILURE — Duke 化療預測醜聞 (Potti et al. 2006 Nat Med)：Potti 與 Nevins 在 Nat Med 發表「gene-expression 預測化療反應」，部分基於該模型啟動臨床試驗。Baggerly & Coombes 2009 Ann Appl Stat 用「forensic bioinformatics」逐字重做：發現訓練集／驗證集 row 對齊錯、heatmap label off-by-one、原 code 從未公開。最終 11 篇 paper 撤稿，3 個 NIH 臨床試驗中止，Potti 失去 Duke 教職。教訓：未公開 code/data 的可重現性災難會吞噬整個職涯。
FAMOUS FAILURE — Duke chemotherapy-prediction scandal (Potti et al. 2006 Nat Med): Potti and Nevins published a gene-expression model predicting chemotherapy response in Nat Med; clinical trials launched on its basis. Baggerly & Coombes 2009 Ann Appl Stat performed "forensic bioinformatics" — they found row-misalignment between training and validation sets, off-by-one heatmap labels, and code never released. Eleven papers were retracted, three NIH trials halted, Potti lost his Duke faculty position. Lesson: a reproducibility disaster from undisclosed code/data can swallow an entire career.
⚠️ 失敗
陷阱

6 個 Methods 典型陷阱

陷阱 1：含糊「我們用 Seurat 做 clustering」

無版本、無 resolution、無 PCs、無 seed = 等同於沒寫。Seurat v4→v5 的 SCTransform、Louvain 預設值差異足以改變 cluster 邊界。

修法：Seurat v5.1.0 + resolution=0.6 + top 30 PCs + random seed=42。

No version, no resolution, no PCs, no seed = unwritten. Seurat v4 vs v5 SCTransform / Louvain defaults differ enough to shift cluster boundaries.

Fix: Seurat v5.1.0 + resolution=0.6 + top 30 PCs + random seed=42.

陷阱 2：缺隨機種子

UMAP、t-SNE、k-means、train-test split、bootstrap 都依賴隨機過程。沒 set.seed() = 同一份 code 第二次跑出來圖不一樣。

修法：R set.seed(42)、Python np.random.seed(42) + torch.manual_seed(42)。

UMAP, t-SNE, k-means, train-test split, bootstrap all rely on stochastic steps. Without set.seed(), the same code yields different plots next time.

Fix: R set.seed(42); Python np.random.seed(42) + torch.manual_seed(42).

陷阱 3：「Data available upon request」當免責牌

Gabelica 2022 顯示只有 6.7% 真的會分享。當你沒打算 deposit 就不要寫——直接用 controlled access (dbGaP) 或標示「將於 N 個月內 deposit」。

修法：論文接受日 deposit + DOI；無法公開時用 controlled access + accession。

Gabelica 2022 showed only 6.7% actually share. If you don't plan to deposit, don't write it — use controlled access (dbGaP) or "will deposit within N months."

Fix: Deposit at acceptance + DOI; if can't go public, use controlled access + accession.

陷阱 4：缺 IRB / IACUC 編號

沒有編號 = 期刊 desk-reject。臨床／動物研究的所有層級都必須核准號 + Declaration of Helsinki (2024 revision) 引用。

修法：Ethics section 第一行寫上 approval no. XXX-2025-001 + 引用 Declaration of Helsinki / ARRIVE 2.0。

No number = journal desk-reject. Any human / animal study tier requires an approval number + Declaration of Helsinki (2024 revision) citation.

Fix: Ethics section opens with approval no. XXX-2025-001 + cite Declaration of Helsinki / ARRIVE 2.0.

陷阱 5：未揭露多重分析 / p-hacking

跑了 12 種 cutoff 只報 p<0.05 那一種 = p-hacking。即使無心，也應交代所有嘗試。

修法：列出所有 cut-off 與 sensitivity analyses，並用 BH-FDR 或 pre-registration 鎖死 primary endpoint。

Tested 12 cutoffs and reported only the p<0.05 one = p-hacking. Even unintentional — disclose all attempts.

Fix: List every cutoff and sensitivity analysis; lock primary endpoint with BH-FDR or pre-registration.

陷阱 6：事後 (post-hoc) 計算樣本量

實驗做完才反算 power = 套套邏輯 (Hoenig & Heisey 2001 Am Stat)，毫無資訊量。樣本量計算必須事前。

修法：實驗前用 G*Power / pwr 計算，公開設定參數；實驗後若樣本不足，誠實在 Limitations 交代。

Computing power after the experiment = tautology (Hoenig & Heisey 2001 Am Stat); zero information. Sample size must be computed a priori.

Fix: Pre-experiment with G*Power / pwr, publish parameters; if underpowered, honestly disclose in Limitations.

Introduction 與 Discussion 會因為時代改變而過時，但方法是論文唯一不會被時間打折的部分——只要寫得夠詳細，10 年後仍能被引用、被重做、被擴充。

必備子段

二、Methods 的 6 個子段

Study design & setting

研究設計（cohort / RCT / cross-sectional）、時間（recruitment dates）、地點（醫院、實驗室）、是否多中心。第一句就要清楚交代，避免讀者腦補。

Design (cohort / RCT / cross-sectional), period (recruitment dates), site (hospital, lab), single- or multi-center. Make the first sentence unambiguous.

Participants / samples

Inclusion / exclusion criteria 必須完整列出。臨床研究：年齡、性別、診斷準則。生資/Omics：物種、組織、批次資訊、樣本來源（GEO accession、collaborator）。

Inclusion / exclusion criteria — fully spelled out. Clinical: age, sex, diagnostic criteria. Bioinformatics/omics: species, tissue, batch info, accession (GEO/SRA) or collaborator source.

Variables / measurements

每個變項：定義、單位、量測工具、測量者、reliability。Primary outcome 與 secondary outcomes 分開列。

Per variable: definition, unit, instrument, measurer, reliability. List primary outcome and secondary outcomes separately.

Sample size & power

α、β、effect size、預期 dropout、最後算出 n。引用所用軟體（G*Power、PASS、R pwr）。RCT 與 ML 模型尤其必要。

α, β, effect size, expected dropout, final n. Cite software (G*Power, PASS, R pwr). Mandatory for RCTs and ML models.

Statistical / computational analysis

所用統計檢定 + 假設檢驗條件、效應量、CI、缺失值處理、敏感度分析。生資：完整 pipeline、所有套件版本、隨機種子。

Statistical tests + assumptions, effect sizes, CIs, missing-data handling, sensitivity analyses. Bioinformatics: full pipeline, all package versions, random seeds.

Ethics, code & data availability

IRB/IACUC 編號、informed consent、利益衝突；data deposit (GEO / Zenodo / Figshare) + DOI；code repository (GitHub) + commit hash + license。

IRB/IACUC number, informed consent, COI; data deposit (GEO / Zenodo / Figshare) + DOI; code repo (GitHub) + commit hash + license.

歷史小註：「Methods 章節」並非自始存在。17 世紀 Galileo 與 Kepler 通信中已有 proto-Methods 式的步驟描述（例如望遠鏡鏡片打磨方法），但正式區隔出 Methods 段落要等到 19 世紀末科學期刊標準化——Rudolf Virchow 編 Archiv 1847 開始要求作者交代材料來源與切片方法。20 世紀中期 American Psychological Association 1929 Publication Manual 首度將 "Method" 列為 paper 的必備節，催生今日 IMRaD 結構。1948 Streptomycin RCT 之後，「Methods 須交代隨機分派與盲化」成為臨床研究金標。到 2008 EQUATOR 成立，Methods 寫作從「個人風格」轉為「跨期刊標準」。2026 年的 AI 揭露要求，可視為 Methods 段持續演化的最新一章。
Historical note: The "Methods section" wasn't always a thing. Letters between Galileo and Kepler in the 17th century contained proto-Methods step descriptions (e.g. how to grind a telescope lens), but a formal Methods section had to wait for 19th-century journal standardization — Rudolf Virchow's Archiv (1847) began demanding that authors disclose specimen sources and sectioning methods. The American Psychological Association's 1929 Publication Manual was the first to list "Method" as a required section, seeding today's IMRaD. After the 1948 Streptomycin RCT, "Methods must disclose randomization and blinding" became the clinical-research gold standard. With EQUATOR's founding in 2008, Methods writing moved from "personal style" to "cross-journal standard." The 2026 AI-disclosure requirements are the latest chapter in the Methods section's ongoing evolution.
