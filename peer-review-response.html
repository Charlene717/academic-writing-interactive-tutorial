故事開場

場景：Major Revision 第一天的怒氣

SCENE · 2025 · Riken Lab

從生氣到分類：Aiko 的 6 週重生

博士生 Aiko 在投稿三個月後收到 decision letter——Major Revision。第一次讀 reviewer 2 的 comments，她又氣又委屈：「他根本沒看懂我的 Figure 3！」、「他要求的實驗根本超出範圍！」她當場想寫回信反駁。

PI 看到她在敲鍵盤，輕輕說：「等 24 小時。明天再看一次。」

第二天 Aiko 再讀一次，發現大部分意見其實合理：reviewer 1 點出 cluster 解析度沒做敏感度分析、reviewer 2 提出的對照組確實她們從一開始就該設、reviewer 3 要求的統計檢定 5 分鐘就能補上。她把每條意見分成 (a) 容易改、(b) 需新分析/實驗、(c) 可以解釋的不同意、(d) 無法修改。

6 週後，Aiko 完成 4 個新分析 + 1 個新對照組實驗 + 1 份 18 頁的 point-by-point response letter。論文被接受了。她後來說：「最重要的不是回信技巧，是那 24 小時。」

PhD student Aiko receives her decision letter three months after submission — Major Revision. Reading Reviewer 2's comments for the first time, she is furious and hurt: "He didn't even understand my Figure 3!" "His requested experiment is way out of scope!" She opens a reply window to push back.

Her PI sees her typing and gently says: "Wait 24 hours. Read it again tomorrow."

The next day Aiko re-reads. Most criticisms are reasonable: Reviewer 1 rightly asks for a cluster-resolution sensitivity analysis; Reviewer 2's requested control should have been there from day one; Reviewer 3's stat test takes 5 minutes. She buckets every comment into (a) easy fix, (b) needs new analysis/experiment, (c) explainable disagreement, (d) cannot be done.

Six weeks later: 4 new analyses, 1 new control experiment, an 18-page point-by-point response letter. The paper is accepted. Aiko later says: "The biggest skill wasn't writing — it was those 24 hours."

核心詞彙

14 個關鍵術語（雙版本）

每個詞有兩個版本：🧒「給小孩看」用比喻、📐「給研究者看」給定義。

Each term has two versions: 🧒 "for kids" uses a metaphor; 📐 "for researchers" gives the formal definition.

Peer review
同儕審查
把作業給其他同行批改——他們挑出錯誤，你再改一次。
Letting other classmates grade your homework — they find errors, you fix them.
由獨立同領域專家評估稿件之科學嚴謹度、原創性與重要性的程序；現代學術出版的核心品質把關機制。
Independent assessment of manuscript rigor, novelty, and significance by experts in the field; the core quality-control mechanism of modern scholarly publishing.
Single-blind
單盲審查
審稿人知道你是誰，你不知道審稿人是誰。
Reviewers know who you are; you don't know who they are.
最常見模式 (60-70% 期刊)；reviewers 看得到作者資訊，作者看不到 reviewer 身份。可能引入 prestige bias。
Most common (60-70% of journals); reviewers see authors, not vice versa. Risk: prestige bias.
Double-blind
雙盲審查
兩邊都看不到對方是誰——像匿名投票。
Neither side sees the other — like anonymous voting.
作者與 reviewer 互相匿名；可減少 gender、institution、country bias，但 self-citation 仍可能暴露身份。
Authors and reviewers mutually anonymous; reduces gender/institution/country bias, though self-citation can leak identity.
Open peer review
公開審查
所有人都看得到審稿評論——而且通常會署名。
Everyone can read the review — and reviewers often sign their names.
eLife、F1000Research、BMJ 採用；reviewer 簽名、評論公開、提升透明度但可能讓 reviewer 不敢嚴格批評。
Used by eLife, F1000Research, BMJ; reviewers sign, reviews are public — boosts transparency but may soften criticism.
Reviewer
審稿人
幫期刊把關的同行專家——通常 2-4 位、義務性、不領薪。
Volunteer experts who screen papers for the journal — usually 2-4, unpaid.
由 editor 邀請、領域內具相關發表的研究者；提交 confidential 評論 + 給 editor 的建議 (accept/revise/reject)。
Invited by editor; field-relevant published researchers; submit confidential comments + recommendation to editor.
Editor
主編／責任編輯
期刊的「裁判」——綜合審稿人意見後做最終決定。
The journal's "referee" — makes the final call after weighing reviews.
分為 Editor-in-Chief、Handling Editor、Associate Editor；可不採納 reviewer 建議；在 COI、citation coercion 案件中扮演仲裁角色。
Roles: Editor-in-Chief / Handling / Associate; may override reviewer recommendations; arbiter on COI and citation-coercion disputes.
Decision letter
決定信
期刊寫給你「我們要不要刊登」的官方回信。
The journal's official letter saying "we will / won't / maybe publish".
5 種典型決定：Accept、Minor Revision、Major Revision、Reject & Resubmit、Reject；附 reviewer comments + editor 摘要。
Five typical decisions: Accept / Minor / Major / Reject-Resubmit / Reject; includes reviewer comments + editor summary.
Major revision
大修
「有機會、但要做不少額外工作」——通常 2-3 個月內回稿。
"Real chance, but significant work" — usually 2-3 months to resubmit.
需新實驗、新分析或大幅改寫；典型 ~50-70% 最終接受率；務必同時提交 clean + tracked-changes 兩版。
Requires new experiments, analyses, or major rewriting; typical ~50-70% final acceptance; submit both clean + tracked-changes versions.
Minor revision
小修
「基本接受了，只是要修小毛病」——2 週內搞定。
"Basically accepted — just clean up details" — finish within 2 weeks.
文字 / 圖表 / 統計 細節調整；95%+ 最終接受率；仍須完整 point-by-point response。
Wording, figure, or statistics tweaks; 95%+ final acceptance; still requires a full point-by-point response.
Point-by-point response
逐點回覆
把審稿人每一句意見「一條一條」回——不能跳過任何一條。
Reply to every comment one by one — skipping any is forbidden.
標準 4 元素：引用 reviewer 原話 → 回應 → 文中改了哪裡 (頁/行) → 必要時附新數據。
Standard 4 elements: quote reviewer verbatim → response → where changed (page/line) → new data if needed.
Cover letter
投稿信／回覆封面信
寄回稿子時夾在前面的一封短信——「我們做了哪些大改」。
A short letter on top of the resubmission — "here's the big stuff we changed".
寫給 editor，1 頁；列出 reviewer 主要關注、總結 revision 方向；不要重複 point-by-point 細節。
Addressed to editor, 1 page; summarizes main reviewer concerns and revision strategy; don't duplicate point-by-point details.
Reviewer comment
審稿意見
審稿人寫的「我覺得這篇怎樣」的具體建議或質疑。
A reviewer's specific critique or suggestion about the paper.
分 Major / Minor / Confidential to editor 三類；必須全部回覆，confidential 部分 editor 通常會摘給作者。
Three categories: Major / Minor / Confidential to editor; respond to all visible ones; editor usually summarizes confidential parts.
Response letter
回覆信
回稿時附上的「我們怎麼回每一條」總文件——通常 10-30 頁。
The master document of "how we addressed each comment" — usually 10-30 pages.
= Cover letter + Point-by-point response 合集；reviewer 與 editor 都會仔細閱讀，是說服力關鍵。
= Cover letter + Point-by-point response combined; both reviewers and editor read carefully — the key persuasion document.
Citation coercion
引用脅迫
審稿人逼你「引用我的論文」——學術倫理紅線。
A reviewer pressuring you to "cite my own papers" — a clear ethics red line.
Wilhite & Fong 2012 Science 揭露；COPE 規範禁止；若 reviewer 強制要求引用其論文且與內容無關，可向 editor 申訴。
Exposed by Wilhite & Fong 2012 Science; banned by COPE; report to editor if a reviewer demands unrelated self-citations.

Σ-逐步流程

從收到 reviewer comments 到送回的 8 步

Σ₁
等 24 小時
關掉信箱、做別的事。第一次讀 comments 的情緒反應通常 90% 是錯的判斷。Close the inbox, do something else. 90% of first-read emotional reactions are wrong judgments.
Σ₂
建立 spreadsheet：每條 comment 分類
欄位：reviewer #、comment 編號、原話、分類 (a/b/c/d)、估計時間、誰負責。Columns: reviewer #, comment #, verbatim text, bucket (a/b/c/d), time estimate, owner.
Σ₃
30 分鐘 co-author 策略會議
確認哪些 (b) 真要做、哪些 (c) 怎麼回；得到 PI 與資深作者的書面同意。Decide which (b) to actually do, how to handle (c); get written sign-off from PI and senior authors.
Σ₄
排時程：(a) 1 週、(b) 4-8 週
標出 deadline 倒推；新實驗預留 buffer (試劑、儀器排程)。Reverse from deadline; leave buffer for new experiments (reagents, instrument booking).
Σ₅
先把 (a)+(b) 做完，再開始寫
不要邊做邊寫——新數據可能改變你對 (c) 的判斷與整體 narrative。Don't write while running — new data may shift your judgment on (c) and the overall narrative.
Σ₆
寫 point-by-point response：4 元素結構
引用 reviewer 原話 → 我們的回應 → 文中改了哪裡 (頁碼/行號) → 必要時附新 Figure/Table。Quote reviewer verbatim → our response → location of change (page/line) → new Figure/Table if needed.
Σ₇
Co-author 校稿 clean + tracked-changes 兩版
兩版都要送：clean 版供印刷、tracked-changes 版供 reviewer 比對。Submit both: clean version for typesetting, tracked-changes version for reviewer comparison.
Σ₈
提早 3-5 天送回
系統可能塞車、PDF 編譯出錯、ORCID 連結失效——預留緩衝。Systems lag, PDFs fail to compile, ORCID links break — leave buffer.
專家辯論

3 個 reviewer 倫理爭議

(a) Reviewer 要求過多新實驗——該推回嗎？

挺推回派：COPE 2017 指引明訂「reviewer 不應要求超出論文 scope 的新工作」。若 reviewer 要求重做整篇分析、補 N=100 動物實驗、新增 6 個 cohort，可在 cover letter 中禮貌請 editor 仲裁。

反推回派：多數情況新實驗會「真的」讓論文變強。30% 的「過多要求」事後看來是合理的——只是當下感覺工程量大。先估算時間：若 ≤8 週、做完更有說服力，就做。

共識：列「做了哪些 / 為何其他不做」由 editor 判斷；不要單方面拒絕也不要全盤接受。

Push-back camp: COPE 2017 guidelines state reviewers shouldn't request work beyond paper scope. If a reviewer asks to redo all analyses, add N=100 animal experiments, or 6 new cohorts, politely ask the editor to arbitrate in the cover letter.

Do-the-work camp: Most "excessive" requests genuinely strengthen the paper. ~30% of "too much" requests look reasonable in hindsight — they just feel daunting now. Estimate time: if ≤8 weeks and improves persuasiveness, do it.

Consensus: List "what we did / why we didn't do the rest" and let editor judge; don't unilaterally reject, don't blindly accept everything.

(b) Citation coercion——reviewer 要求引用他自己的論文

Wilhite & Fong 2012 Science 調查：~14% reviewer 曾要求過自我引用。COPE 規範：若被引論文確實與內容相關，加上是合理的；若與內容無關只是「請引用 (Smith 2019, 2021, 2023)」，這是 citation coercion。

應對：在 response letter 中分項評估每篇被要求的論文——相關者加、不相關者禮貌說明為何不加。若 reviewer 不死心，直接向 handling editor 申訴。Frontiers 2023 已建置 anti-coercion algorithm 自動偵測。

Wilhite & Fong 2012 Science: ~14% of reviewers had pressured self-citation. COPE rules: relevant suggested cites are fine; "please cite (Smith 2019, 2021, 2023)" with no relevance is citation coercion.

What to do: evaluate each suggested paper in response letter — cite the relevant, politely explain why not for the others. If reviewer keeps pushing, appeal to handling editor. Frontiers 2023 deploys an anti-coercion algorithm to flag this.

(c) AI 審稿——Cell/NEJM 禁止，但如何執行？

2023 後多家頂刊 (Cell、Nature、NEJM、Science) 明文禁止把 unpublished manuscript 上傳 ChatGPT/Claude 等 LLM——違反 confidentiality。但實際上：reviewer 在家用個人 ChatGPT account 把 PDF 內容貼上去，期刊如何偵測？

執行困境：多數依賴 reviewer 簽署 confidentiality agreement + 自律；少數期刊 (Cabells、ICML 2024) 嘗試 detection tools 反向偵測 AI 文體。

實務建議：作者方可請 editor 透露 reviewer 是否簽署 no-AI 條款；reviewer 方應「只用 AI 校對自己已寫的評論文字」，絕不把作者原稿上傳。

Post-2023, top journals (Cell, Nature, NEJM, Science) explicitly ban uploading unpublished manuscripts to ChatGPT/Claude — violates confidentiality. In practice: a reviewer pastes the PDF into a personal ChatGPT at home — how does the journal know?

Enforcement gap: mostly self-policed via reviewer confidentiality agreements; a few journals (Cabells, ICML 2024) deploy detection tools that flag AI writing patterns.

Practical guide: authors can ask editor whether reviewers signed a no-AI clause; reviewers should "use AI only to polish their own already-written comments" — never upload author manuscript content.

模範與警訊

2 個 spotlight

MODEL · 2022-
eLife / Sciety 公開審查平台

eLife 從 2023 改採「publish, then review」模式：作者投稿後若 editor 認為有意義，立即以 preprint 形式發表 + 公開的 reviewer 評估。所有評論都可在 sciety.org 上閱覽，並接受社群進一步討論。

影響：reviewer 評論變成「可被後人查核」的學術產出，類似真正的 scholarly output；reviewer 也可在自己 CV 中放公開 review 連結。Nature 2024 報導：eLife 模式正在被 BMJ Open Science、F1000 等期刊評估採用。

Since 2023 eLife adopts a "publish, then review" model: once editor judges meaningful, papers go up as preprints with public reviewer assessments. All reviews are visible on sciety.org and open to community discussion.

Impact: reviewer comments become "auditable" scholarly outputs — reviewers can cite public reviews on their own CV. Nature 2024 reports: BMJ Open Science, F1000 evaluating similar models.

WARNING · 2014
peer-review ring 醜聞

2014 年 SAGE Publishing 的《Journal of Vibration and Control》撤稿 60 篇論文——調查發現一群研究者建立 fake reviewer email 帳號、互相審查彼此論文，形成「審稿環」 (peer-review ring)。為期數年、影響數十篇論文，最終總編 Ali Nayfeh 辭職。

教訓：單盲 + 作者可建議 reviewer 的制度可被濫用。現代防範：editor 須核對 reviewer 的真實 institutional email、跨期刊 reviewer database (Publons / WoS Reviewer Academy)、相同 IP 警示。

In 2014 SAGE Publishing's J. Vibration and Control retracted 60 papers — investigation found a group of researchers set up fake reviewer email accounts, reviewing each other's papers in a "peer-review ring." Lasted years, affected dozens of papers; editor-in-chief Ali Nayfeh resigned.

Lesson: single-blind + author-suggested-reviewer system is exploitable. Modern safeguards: editors verify reviewer institutional emails, cross-journal reviewer databases (Publons / WoS Reviewer Academy), and same-IP flagging.

陷阱清單

6 個典型陷阱

1. 情緒化反駁
「Reviewer 根本沒看懂」、「這要求太離譜」——任何帶情緒的字眼都會讓 editor 站到 reviewer 那邊。"Reviewer doesn't understand", "This is absurd" — any emotional word pushes editor onto the reviewer's side.
2. 跳過 comments
即使是「明顯不重要」的 minor comment 也要回——跳過任何一條都是 reviewer 給 editor 的紅燈訊號。Even "obviously trivial" minor comments must get replies — skipping any is a red flag the reviewer flashes to the editor.
3. 「Done」沒細節
只寫「已修改」、「謝謝建議」沒指出頁碼/行號——reviewer 找不到改在哪。Just writing "Done" or "Thanks for the suggestion" without page/line — reviewer can't locate the change.
4. 沒附 tracked-changes
只送 clean version——reviewer 必須逐行重讀整篇對比，幾乎一定不爽。Submitting only clean version — reviewer must re-read line by line and compare; almost certainly annoyed.
5. response 缺頁/行 ref
「我們已在 Discussion 補強」——是哪一段？哪一行？reviewer 不應自行找。"We strengthened the Discussion" — which paragraph? line? Reviewer shouldn't have to hunt.
6. 撤稿而非修改
收到 Major Revision 就撤稿改投他家——浪費了 3 位 reviewer 的免費高質意見，下一家還要從頭審。Withdrawing after Major Revision and submitting elsewhere — wastes 3 reviewers' free high-quality feedback; next journal must start from scratch.
正反對照

同一條 comment：壞回覆 vs 好回覆

情境：reviewer 質疑「為何選 cluster resolution 0.6？沒做敏感度分析」。

Scenario: reviewer asks "why resolution 0.6? No sensitivity analysis."

❌ 壞回覆

"Reviewer doesn't understand. We've done extensive analysis already and the resolution choice was based on standard practice in the field."

"Reviewer doesn't understand. We've done extensive analysis already and the resolution choice was based on standard practice in the field."

  • 用「不懂」直接攻擊 reviewer
  • 沒有引用 reviewer 原話
  • 沒附新數據
  • 沒指出文中哪裡改
  • Attacks reviewer with "doesn't understand"
  • Doesn't quote reviewer verbatim
  • No new data
  • No page/line reference
✅ 好回覆

"Reviewer comment: '為何選 cluster resolution 0.6？沒做敏感度分析。'"

"We thank the reviewer for this important point. We systematically tested resolutions 0.2–1.2 in increments of 0.1 (new Supplementary Figure S4a). Cluster stability measured by Adjusted Rand Index (ARI = 0.91) plateaued at resolution ≥0.6, supporting our original choice. We added the rationale on page 7, lines 12–18, and reference Supp Fig S4."

"Reviewer comment: 'Why resolution 0.6? No sensitivity analysis.'"

"We thank the reviewer for this important point. We systematically tested resolutions 0.2–1.2 in increments of 0.1 (new Supplementary Figure S4a). Cluster stability (ARI = 0.91) plateaued at resolution ≥0.6, supporting our original choice. We added the rationale on page 7, lines 12–18, and reference Supp Fig S4."

  • 逐字引用 reviewer 原話
  • 感謝 + 具體新分析 (resolution sweep)
  • 量化指標 (ARI = 0.91)
  • 明確頁碼/行號 + 新 Supp Figure
  • Quotes reviewer verbatim
  • Thanks + concrete new analysis (resolution sweep)
  • Quantified metric (ARI = 0.91)
  • Explicit page/line + new Supp Figure
自我檢查

6 個回覆審稿前的自問

  1. 你有先等 24 小時、讓自己冷靜下來再開始寫回信嗎？
  2. Did you wait 24 hours and cool down before starting to write?
  3. 你有把所有 comments 分成 (a) 容易改 / (b) 需新分析 / (c) 可解釋的不同意 / (d) 無法做 四類嗎？
  4. Have you classified all comments into 4 buckets: (a) easy fix / (b) needs new analysis / (c) explainable disagreement / (d) can't do?
  5. 每條回覆都包含 4 元素（引用原話 / 回應 / 文中位置 / 新數據）嗎？
  6. Does each reply contain the 4 elements (quote / response / location / new data)?
  7. 你有同時準備 clean + tracked-changes 兩個版本嗎？
  8. Have you prepared both clean and tracked-changes versions?
  9. 所有 co-author 都看過 response letter 並書面同意了嗎？
  10. Have all co-authors reviewed and signed off on the response letter?
  11. 你保留了至少 3-5 天的提交緩衝時間嗎？
  12. Have you left at least 3-5 days of submission buffer before the deadline?
進階 2024-2026 SOTA

進階：reviewer 訓練與 AI 審稿前沿

(a) Web of Science Reviewer Academy（前身 Publons）免費認證

Clarivate 於 2022 將 Publons 整併入 Web of Science Reviewer Academy。提供免費的 reviewer 訓練模組（共 5 部分，~10 小時）涵蓋如何寫建設性意見、避免偏見、識別 citation coercion。完成後獲得「Certified Reviewer」徽章可連到 ORCID。截至 2025，全球已有 ~120 萬位認證 reviewer。

Clarivate folded Publons into the Web of Science Reviewer Academy in 2022. Free reviewer-training modules (5 parts, ~10 hours) cover writing constructive comments, avoiding bias, and recognizing citation coercion. Completion yields a "Certified Reviewer" badge linkable to ORCID. As of 2025, ~1.2 million certified reviewers globally.

(b) ASAPbio open peer-review trainee programme 2024-26

ASAPbio 與 eLife、PLOS、Review Commons 合作推動博士生 / 博後參與「公開審稿」訓練計畫——挑選 bioRxiv preprint、撰寫公開審稿、由資深 reviewer 一對一指導。2024-26 已超過 500 位 trainee 完訓，所有公開審稿都附 ORCID 並可作為求職 / fellowship 申請的學術產出。

ASAPbio partners with eLife, PLOS, Review Commons to train PhD / postdoc trainees in public reviewing — pick a bioRxiv preprint, write a public review, get one-on-one mentoring from senior reviewers. Over 500 trainees completed 2024-26; reviews are ORCID-linked and usable as scholarly outputs for job / fellowship applications.

(c) AI-augmented review 爭議：Nature/Cell 禁止 vs Cabells 採用

2023-26 兩派分歧加劇：Nature、Cell、NEJM、Science、JAMA 明文禁止把 unpublished manuscript 上傳 LLM；Cabells、Frontiers、MDPI 部分期刊嘗試以 AI 進行初步篩選 (plagiarism、statistics check、scope match)。ICML 2024 爆發爭議——有 reviewer 公開承認使用 GPT-4 輔助但未揭露，引發政策大改。共識浮現：「AI 可協助 reviewer 校對自己已寫的評論，但不可接觸原稿」。

2023-26: divide intensifies. Nature, Cell, NEJM, Science, JAMA explicitly ban uploading unpublished manuscripts to LLMs; Cabells, Frontiers, some MDPI journals trial AI screening (plagiarism, stats check, scope match). ICML 2024 controversy: reviewer publicly admitted using GPT-4 without disclosure, triggering major policy revisions. Consensus emerging: "AI may help reviewers polish their own already-written comments, but must never touch the manuscript itself."

歷史小註

同儕審查比大學年輕

很多人以為「同儕審查」與科學一樣古老，事實上完全不是。1665 年 Royal Society 創刊《Philosophical Transactions》時，所謂的「審查」只是學者之間私下寄信討論——沒有制度、沒有匿名、沒有正式 referee 報告。

真正的「匿名雙盲」現代制度直到 1930 年代才在物理、醫學領域逐步成形——比哈佛大學 (1636)、牛津大學 (1096) 都晚了好幾百年。換句話說：peer review 比大多數大學還年輕。當你在思考「是否該質疑這個 reviewer 的要求」時，記得：這套制度本身也還在演化中。

Many people assume peer review is as ancient as science itself. Not at all. When the Royal Society launched Philosophical Transactions in 1665, "review" was casual letters exchanged between scholars — no formal system, no anonymity, no referee reports.

The modern "anonymous double-blind" system only crystallized in the 1930s in physics and medicine — centuries after Harvard (1636) and Oxford (1096). In other words: peer review is younger than most universities. When you wonder whether to push back on a reviewer demand, remember: the system itself is still evolving.

概覽

收到 comments 的第一件事

一：休息 24 小時。第一次讀 comments 大多會生氣。讓自己冷靜後再回頭——你會發現 80% 的批評是有道理的。

二：分類 comments。用 spreadsheet 把每位 reviewer 的每點分為：(a) 同意 + 容易改、(b) 同意 + 需新實驗/分析、(c) 不同意但能解釋、(d) 不能改 (技術上 / 範圍上)。

三：寫 point-by-point response.每點都要回，逐字引用 reviewer 原話 → 我們的回應 → 文中改了哪裡（頁碼/行號）。

1. Take 24 hours. First read = anger. Cool down, come back — you'll find 80% of critiques are reasonable.

2. Classify comments. Use a spreadsheet to bucket each reviewer comment: (a) agree + easy fix, (b) agree + new experiment/analysis needed, (c) disagree but explainable, (d) can't fix (technical / scope).

3. Write point-by-point response. Every point gets a reply: quote reviewer verbatim → our response → where we changed (page/line).

But improve based on reviewer comments before resubmitting, or the next journal will reject for the same reason.
Response Letter 結構

二、Response Letter 結構

總覽信

感謝 editor 與 reviewers 時間 + 1 段 highlights：「我們新增了 3 個實驗、增加了 1 個 cohort 驗證、重做了 statistical analysis。所有 comments 已逐點回應。」

Thank editor + reviewers + 1 paragraph of highlights: "We added 3 new experiments, included 1 validation cohort, redid the statistics. All comments addressed point-by-point."

Reviewer 1 逐點回應

每點 4 元素：(1) 引用 reviewer 原話 (italic) → (2) 我們的 response → (3) 文中改動 (含頁碼/行號) → (4) 必要時引新數據/圖。

Each point has 4 elements: (1) quote reviewer (italic) → (2) our response → (3) manuscript changes (page/line) → (4) cite new data/figures as needed.

Reviewer 2, 3...

每位 reviewer 獨立 section。即使 R2 跟 R1 同問題，也分別回應 (但可寫 "as detailed in our response to R1")。

Each reviewer in its own section. Even if R2 asks the same as R1, reply separately (you can write "as detailed in our response to R1").

改動 manuscript

附上 (a) clean version + (b) tracked-changes / coloured version。讓 editor 一眼看出修改範圍。

Attach (a) clean version + (b) tracked-changes / coloured version so editor sees changes at a glance.

範例對照

三、回應對照

❌ 情緒化反駁

「Reviewer 顯然沒理解我們的方法。我們已經做了非常多分析，沒辦法再做更多了。這個批評不公平。」

"The reviewer obviously didn't understand our method. We have done extensive analysis already and cannot do more. This criticism is unfair."

✅ 禮貌 + 有依據

「Reviewer 1, comment 3: 'The clustering resolution of 0.6 seems arbitrary; please justify or test alternatives.'

Response: We thank the reviewer for this important point. We have now systematically tested resolutions from 0.2 to 1.2 in steps of 0.2 (new Supplementary Figure S4). Cluster identity stability (Adjusted Rand Index vs original 0.6) plateaued at resolution ≥0.6, supporting our original choice. We have added the rationale on p.7, lines 12–18 and the comparison plot as Supp Fig S4.」

"Reviewer 1, comment 3: 'The clustering resolution of 0.6 seems arbitrary; please justify or test alternatives.'

Response: We thank the reviewer for this important point. We systematically tested resolutions 0.2 to 1.2 in steps of 0.2 (new Supplementary Figure S4). Cluster identity stability (Adjusted Rand Index vs the original 0.6) plateaued at ≥0.6, supporting our choice. Rationale added on p.7, lines 12–18, with the comparison plot as Supp Fig S4."

❌ 不同意但無依據

「我們不同意 reviewer 的看法。我們認為原稿已經夠了。」

"We disagree with the reviewer. We believe the original manuscript is sufficient."

✅ 禮貌不同意 + 依據

「我們感謝 reviewer 的意見，但對此點我們有不同看法。原因：(1) 我們原文 p.5 已說明 SCTransform v2 是經過 benchmark 推薦的方法 (Choudhary & Satija, 2022)；(2) 我們在新 Supp Fig S5 提供 LogNormalize vs SCTransform 的並排比較，cluster 結構幾乎一致 (ARI=0.91)。我們希望這已充分回應 reviewer 的疑慮，但若 reviewer 認為仍需更換，我們也願意配合。」

"We thank the reviewer but respectfully disagree on this point. Reasons: (1) p.5 already cites Choudhary & Satija (2022) showing SCTransform v2 is the benchmarked recommendation; (2) we now provide a side-by-side LogNormalize vs SCTransform comparison in Supp Fig S5 — cluster structure is nearly identical (ARI=0.91). We hope this addresses the concern, but are happy to switch methods if the reviewer considers it necessary."

當審稿人時

四、輪到你當 reviewer

大多數研究者在投稿 5-10 篇後會被邀請當 reviewer。好的 review 既能幫作者改善論文、也能訓練自己批判性思維。Reviewer 訓練資源：Publons Academy, ASAPbio Reviewer Trainee Programme, Sense About Science。

結構建議：

  1. Summary (1 段)：用自己話總結作者做了什麼（讓 editor / authors 確認你看懂）。
  2. Major points (5-10 條)：影響核心結論的批評，分點編號。
  3. Minor points (5-15 條)：typos, figure issues, citations。
  4. Confidential to editor：對 novelty / fit / scientific misconduct 的隱密意見。

Most researchers are invited as reviewers after 5-10 published papers. A good review both improves the paper and trains your critical thinking. Training: Publons Academy, ASAPbio Reviewer Trainee Programme, Sense About Science.

Recommended structure:

  1. Summary (1 paragraph): in your own words, what the authors did (lets editor + authors confirm you understood).
  2. Major points (5-10): critiques affecting core conclusions; numbered.
  3. Minor points (5-15): typos, figures, citations.
  4. Confidential to editor: private notes on novelty / fit / suspected misconduct.
⚠️
Reviewer 倫理：① 利益衝突 (作者是合作者、競爭者、學生) 必須揭露給 editor；② 不要把稿件分享給未授權者 (學生 OK 但要先告知 editor)；③ 不能用 reviewer 身份要求作者引用自己的論文 ("citation coercion") — 嚴重違反 COPE 規範。
Reviewer ethics: ① conflicts of interest (collaborator, competitor, student) must be disclosed; ② never share the manuscript with unauthorized people (students OK but inform editor first); ③ never demand authors cite your own papers ("citation coercion") — major COPE violation.
