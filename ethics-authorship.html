故事開場

場景：Ben 對「禮物作者」說不

SCENE · 2026 · Lab Meeting

從「PI 加上一位陌生教授」到 Acknowledgements

博士生 Ben 終於要把兩年的 spatial transcriptomics 計畫送投 Nature Methods。PI 把 author list 寄回給他，多出一個名字——系主任 H 教授。Ben 從沒見過他，更別說討論過資料。「他幫過我們申請設備經費，加上吧。」PI 說。

Ben 翻出 ICMJE 4 條件清單：(1) 實質學術貢獻 (2) 撰稿或關鍵修訂 (3) 同意最終版本 (4) 同意 accountability。H 教授一條都沒符合。這是典型的 gift authorship——學術界最常見的紅線之一。

Ben 緊張了三天，最後寄信給 PI：「我認為 H 教授的貢獻屬於 Acknowledgements——『感謝 H 教授協助設備經費取得』。如果掛 author，投稿時 ICMJE COI form 我們填不過去。」PI 沉默 24 小時，回信：「OK，移到 Acknowledgements。」

Paper 半年後被接受。Ben 學到三件事：每位 author 都要有 ORCID、每人的貢獻要照 CRediT 14 角色列出、COI 的 36 個月回溯期不能漏。

PhD student Ben is finally ready to submit his two-year spatial-transcriptomics project to Nature Methods. The PI returns the author list with one extra name — department head Prof. H. Ben has never met him, let alone discussed the data. "He helped us get equipment funding. Add him." says the PI.

Ben pulls up the ICMJE 4 criteria: (1) substantial contribution; (2) drafting or critical revision; (3) approval of final version; (4) accountability. Prof. H meets none of them. This is textbook gift authorship — one of academia's most common red lines.

After three tense days Ben writes to the PI: "I think Prof. H belongs in Acknowledgements — 'We thank Prof. H for help with equipment funding.' If we list him as author, we can't truthfully complete the ICMJE COI form." The PI is silent for 24 hours, then replies: "OK, move to Acknowledgements."

The paper is accepted six months later. Ben has learned three things: every author needs an ORCID; per-author contributions must follow the CRediT 14-role taxonomy; the 36-month COI lookback can't be skipped.

核心詞彙

14 個關鍵術語（雙版本）

每個詞有兩個版本：🧒「給小孩看」用比喻、📐「給研究者看」給定義。

Each term has two versions: 🧒 "for kids" uses a metaphor; 📐 "for researchers" gives the formal definition.

ICMJE
國際醫學期刊編輯委員會
一群醫學期刊主編組成的「規則委員會」，定下誰能掛名、怎麼掛、有什麼利益要揭露。
A "rules committee" of medical-journal editors who set who can be an author, how to list them, and what conflicts to disclose.
International Committee of Medical Journal Editors，1978 年溫哥華成立。其 Recommendations 是 2026 全球生醫期刊的事實標準。
International Committee of Medical Journal Editors, formed in Vancouver 1978. Its Recommendations are the de-facto 2026 global biomedical-journal standard.
ICMJE 4 Criteria
ICMJE 4 條件
當 author 要拿到 4 個章：有貢獻、寫過或改過、同意最終版、願意負責——少一個都不行。
Being an author needs 4 stamps: contributed, drafted/revised, approved, accountable — missing any one disqualifies you.
必須同時符合 ① 實質學術貢獻 ② 撰稿或關鍵修訂 ③ 同意最終版本 ④ accountability。任何一條不符 → 應列 Acknowledgements。
Must satisfy all four: ① substantial contribution; ② drafting/critical revision; ③ approval of final version; ④ accountability. Fail any → list under Acknowledgements.
CRediT taxonomy
CRediT 貢獻角色分類
電影片尾名單——導演、編劇、攝影、剪輯每個職位都列出來，論文也用 14 種角色清楚標記每人。
Like film credits — director, writer, cinematographer all listed; papers list 14 roles per author.
Contributor Roles Taxonomy；NISO 2014 提出，2022 成為 ANSI/NISO Z39.104 標準；14 個角色，每位作者列 1-N 個。
Contributor Roles Taxonomy; NISO 2014, ANSI/NISO Z39.104 since 2022; 14 roles, each author assigned 1–N.
ORCID
ORCID iD
研究者的「身分證號」——16 個數字，全世界只有你一個。
A researcher's "ID number" — 16 digits, globally unique to you.
Open Researcher and Contributor ID；2012 上線；2026 多數主流期刊強制 corresponding author 提供。範例 0000-0002-1825-0097。
Open Researcher and Contributor ID; launched 2012; in 2026 most major publishers mandate it for corresponding authors. Example 0000-0002-1825-0097.
ROR
機構識別碼
機構版的 ORCID——每間大學/醫院有自己唯一編號，避免「台大 vs 台灣大學 vs NTU」三種寫法混亂。
Institutional ORCID — each university/hospital gets a unique code, no more "NTU vs National Taiwan University vs 台大" mix-ups.
Research Organization Registry，由 Crossref/DataCite/CDL 聯合維護；JATS XML 後設資料中與 ORCID 平行使用。
Research Organization Registry, maintained by Crossref/DataCite/CDL; used alongside ORCID in JATS XML metadata.
IRB / IACUC
人體 / 動物倫理審查
做人體或動物研究前要先「過關」的審查委員會——確認你不會傷害受試者。
Pre-experiment "approval board" — confirms you won't harm participants.
Institutional Review Board (human) / Institutional Animal Care and Use Committee (animal)；論文 Methods 必須列出 approval number 與委員會名稱。
Institutional Review Board (human) / Institutional Animal Care and Use Committee (animal); the Methods section must cite the approval number and committee name.
Conflict of Interest (COI)
利益衝突
「你從這研究中能得到錢/股票/職位嗎？」事先講清楚——不是禁止，是必須透明。
"Do you stand to gain money/stock/jobs from this study?" Disclose up front — it's not banned, just must be transparent.
ICMJE COI form：36 個月回溯期，需揭露 funding / consultancy / equity / patents / personal relationships。漏報 = 撤稿風險。
ICMJE COI form: 36-month lookback covering funding, consultancy, equity, patents, personal relationships. Omission risks retraction.
Authorship
作者身分
不只是「在 paper 上面有名字」，而是同時承擔功勞與責任。
It's not just "your name on the paper" — it carries both credit and responsibility.
公開承擔對 published work 的 intellectual ownership 與 accountability；不可拒絕掛名以逃避責任，也不可掛名而無貢獻。
Publicly accepting intellectual ownership and accountability for the work; can neither hide from responsibility nor sit on a byline without contribution.
Gift authorship
禮物作者
「給長官面子加上去」——但他根本沒做這個研究。是學術界最常見、最被默許的紅線。
"Add the senior person to be polite" — but they did nothing on this study. Academia's most common silently-tolerated red line.
把不符 ICMJE 4 條件者掛 author，常見於系主任、資深教授。違反 COPE 與 ICMJE 規範；2024-2026 多家期刊 audit 抽查。
Listing someone who fails ICMJE 4 criteria as author, often department heads/senior faculty. Violates COPE and ICMJE; multiple journals run audits 2024-2026.
Ghost authorship
幽靈作者
「真的寫了 paper 的人卻不掛名」——常見於藥廠付錢請 medical writer 而隱藏其身分。
The person who actually wrote the paper is hidden — common when pharma pays a medical writer but conceals it.
符合 ICMJE author 條件卻被刻意排除；2026 ICMJE 要求顯式揭露 medical-writer 並列 Acknowledgements。
Someone who meets ICMJE criteria but is deliberately omitted; 2026 ICMJE requires explicit disclosure of medical writers in Acknowledgements.
Guest authorship
客座作者
「請大牌客串一下」——掛名提高 paper 知名度但沒實質參與。
"Invite a celebrity to guest-star" — a famous name added to boost prestige with no real involvement.
與 gift authorship 高度重疊，差別在動機：guest = 提高 paper 接受率/知名度；gift = 人情、權力結構。
Heavily overlaps with gift authorship; differs in motive — guest aims to boost acceptance/prestige; gift is courtesy/power.
Salami slicing
切香腸式發表
把一塊大蛋糕切成三小塊賣三次——同一份資料硬切成多篇 paper 灌 publication count。
Slice one cake into three pieces to sell three times — split one dataset into multiple papers to inflate publication count.
將最小可發表單位 (LPU) 過度切割；違反 COPE。判斷準則：研究問題、cohort、結論是否有實質差異。
Carving the work below the least-publishable unit (LPU); violates COPE. Test: do question, cohort, and conclusions substantively differ?
Duplicate publication
重複發表
同一份結果投兩個地方——浪費 reviewer 時間、誤導引用統計。
Submitting the same results twice — wastes reviewer time, distorts citation counts.
同一資料/結論在兩處發表而未交叉引用；包含 covert (含中英雙語版) 與 redundant publication。撤稿主因之一。
Same data/conclusions published twice without cross-citation; includes covert (e.g., bilingual versions) and redundant publication. A leading cause of retraction.
Plagiarism
抄襲（含自我抄襲）
「複製別人的字當自己的」——也包含複製自己以前 paper 的句子卻不引用。
"Copying others' words as your own" — includes recycling your own past sentences without citation.
External plagiarism = 抄他人；self-plagiarism = 重用自己過去 paper 文字而未引用。iThenticate 投稿前查重；>15% 須改寫。
External plagiarism = copying others; self-plagiarism = reusing your own past text uncited. Run iThenticate pre-submission; rewrite if >15%.

Σ-walkthrough

從送 IRB 到投稿前的 8 個步驟

Σ₁
確認所有 author 符合 ICMJE 4 條件。逐個核對：實質貢獻、撰稿/修訂、同意最終版、accountability。不符 → Acknowledgements。
Confirm every author meets all 4 ICMJE criteria. Tick each: substantial contribution, drafting/revision, approval, accountability. Fail any → move to Acknowledgements.
Σ₂
列出每位作者的 CRediT 角色。14 角色挑 1-N 個；同一角色可多人。常見錯誤：所有人都列 "Writing — Original Draft"。
List CRediT roles per author. Choose 1-N of the 14 roles; multiple people may share a role. Common mistake: everyone listed as "Writing — Original Draft".
Σ₃
每位作者註冊 ORCID。免費、5 分鐘完成。Corresponding author 必填，共同作者強烈建議。
Register ORCID for everyone. Free, takes 5 minutes. Mandatory for corresponding author; strongly recommended for all co-authors.
Σ₄
送 IRB / IACUC 並取得 approval number。必須在 data collection 之前取得；retrospective 案例需 waiver。Methods 必引用編號。
Submit IRB / IACUC and obtain approval number. Must be obtained before data collection; retrospective studies need a waiver. Methods must cite the number.
Σ₅
每位作者填 ICMJE COI form。36 個月回溯期，揭露 funding / consultancy / equity / patents / personal relationships。
Each author completes the ICMJE COI form. 36-month lookback covering funding, consultancy, equity, patents, personal relationships.
Σ₆
跑 iThenticate 查重。檢查 external plagiarism 與 self-plagiarism；>15% similarity 需重寫，特別注意 Methods 段。
Run iThenticate similarity check. Screens for external and self-plagiarism; rewrite if >15% similarity, especially in Methods.
Σ₇
確認與過去 paper 無切香腸式重疊。同 cohort 衍生多篇 → 必須在 Methods 標註「previously published in [ref]」，每篇研究問題與結論需實質不同。
Verify no salami-style overlap with prior papers. Multiple papers from one cohort → must flag "previously published in [ref]" in Methods; each must have substantively different question and conclusion.
Σ₈
揭露所有 AI 工具使用。ChatGPT / Claude 用於 drafting / editing / coding 須在 Methods 或 Acknowledgements 標註。AI 不可列作者。
Disclose any AI tool use. ChatGPT / Claude used for drafting / editing / coding must be flagged in Methods or Acknowledgements. AI cannot be an author.
專家辯論

3 個倫理灰區

① 給資深教授「禮物作者」——學界常態還是紅線？

支持方：在華人/東亞學界，給系主任或資深教授掛名是「人情、權力結構」必要的潤滑劑。拒絕 → 影響日後升等、計畫審查。

反對方：明確違反 ICMJE。2024 起多家期刊（Nature 系列、PLOS）開始 audit 抽查；一旦發現可能撤稿。建議：將其貢獻明確列在 Acknowledgements，提供具體說明而非掛名。

For: In East Asian academia, listing a department head or senior faculty is a necessary lubricant of personal favors and power structures. Refusing → may hurt promotions, grant reviews.

Against: Clearly violates ICMJE. Since 2024, multiple journals (Nature family, PLOS) have started audits; discovery can lead to retraction. Suggestion: list the contribution explicitly in Acknowledgements with specific text, not as author.

② CRediT taxonomy：有用工具還是 paperwork 負擔？

支持方：解決「不知道誰做了什麼」的長期問題；幫助 early-career researchers 在 CV 上 documented specific 貢獻，而非僅僅一個 author position。

反對方：14 角色過細，多人共同寫 paper 時填寫繁瑣；不同期刊 CRediT 規範不一致；最終標籤仍由 PI 決定，未必反映實際貢獻。

For: Solves the long-standing "who did what?" problem; helps early-career researchers document specific contributions on their CV beyond a byline position.

Against: 14 roles is granular and tedious when many people share writing; journals apply CRediT inconsistently; ultimately the PI assigns labels, which may not reflect reality.

③ 同 cohort 多篇 paper：salami slicing 還是合理追蹤？

支持方：長期 cohort（如 UK Biobank、Framingham）天然會衍生數百篇 paper，每篇研究問題、outcome、結論皆不同——這是合理 secondary analysis。

反對方：把同一份 RNA-seq 拆成「stemness paper」「inflammation paper」「proliferation paper」三篇，僅切換 gene set 但用同一 cohort 同一方法 → 標準 salami slicing。判斷準則：研究問題、結論是否實質不同。

For: Long-term cohorts (UK Biobank, Framingham) naturally yield hundreds of papers; each has a different question, outcome, conclusion — legitimate secondary analysis.

Against: Splitting one RNA-seq into "stemness paper", "inflammation paper", "proliferation paper" — same cohort, same method, only gene-set swap → textbook salami. Test: are question and conclusion substantively different?

模範與警鐘

兩個 spotlight

🌟 模範：PLOS One 強制 CRediT (2018+)

PLOS One 在 2018 年成為第一家強制要求每篇 paper 逐個作者列 CRediT 14 角色的主流期刊。實施 5 年後，多家研究顯示：作者爭議減少 ~25%；early-career researchers 在 CV 中明確標註 specific role 的比例上升 3 倍；後續被 Elsevier, Springer Nature, Cell Press 採納。

PLOS One became in 2018 the first major publisher to mandate per-author CRediT 14-role listings. Five years on, studies report: authorship disputes dropped ~25%; the share of early-career researchers documenting specific roles on their CV tripled; subsequently adopted by Elsevier, Springer Nature, Cell Press.

⚠️ 警鐘：Schön 醜聞 (2002) — 28 篇撤稿

Hendrik Schön 在 Bell Labs 偽造 organic semiconductor 數據；2002 年遭揭露，28 篇 Nature / Science / Phys Rev 等頂刊 paper 被撤稿。最大教訓不在 Schön 個人，而在共同作者——包含多位資深物理學家——從未驗證原始 raw data。這是 ICMJE 第 4 條 accountability 的歷史警鐘：掛名 = 同負責任，若無能力驗證就不該掛名。

Hendrik Schön at Bell Labs fabricated organic-semiconductor data; exposed in 2002, 28 papers in Nature / Science / Phys Rev top journals were retracted. The biggest lesson isn't Schön — it's the co-authors, including senior physicists who never verified the raw data. A historical reminder of ICMJE criterion 4 (accountability): a byline = shared responsibility; if you can't verify, don't sign.

陷阱

6 個典型陷阱

① 幽靈作者

藥廠付錢請 medical writer 寫 paper 卻不揭露其身分。修正：必須在 Acknowledgements 寫「Medical writing assistance was provided by [name] of [company], funded by [sponsor]」。

Pharma pays a medical writer but conceals it. Fix: Acknowledge "Medical writing assistance provided by [name] of [company], funded by [sponsor]."

② 禮物作者

給系主任、資深教授掛名以求人情。修正：移至 Acknowledgements 並寫明具體貢獻（「協助設備經費取得」等）。

Listing department heads or senior faculty for favor. Fix: Move to Acknowledgements with explicit contribution text ("helped obtain equipment funding" etc.).

③ Salami slicing

同份資料切成多篇灌 publication count。修正：合併成 1 篇完整 paper；若真有不同 angle，必須在 Methods 引用前作並說明差異。

Splitting one dataset to inflate publication count. Fix: Consolidate into one complete paper; if angles really differ, cite the prior paper in Methods and explain the distinction.

④ Methods 重用未自引

Wet-lab protocol 從前篇 paper 整段複製貼上而未引用。修正：改寫 + 自引「as described in [ref]」；或用 STAR Methods 引用 protocols.io。

Pasting wet-lab protocol from a prior paper without citing it. Fix: Rewrite + self-cite "as described in [ref]"; or use STAR Methods and cite protocols.io.

⑤ 漏列 IRB number

人體研究的 paper Methods 沒寫 IRB approval number 與委員會名稱。修正：固定句型「This study was approved by [Committee], approval number [XXX]; informed consent was obtained from all participants」。

Human-subject paper omits IRB approval number in Methods. Fix: Use the template "This study was approved by [Committee], approval number [XXX]; informed consent was obtained from all participants."

⑥ 將 AI 列為 co-author

把 ChatGPT / Claude 列為 author。修正：ICMJE 2026 明確禁止；AI 無法承擔 accountability。改在 Methods / Acknowledgements 標註「ChatGPT-5 was used for language editing / code prototyping」。

Listing ChatGPT / Claude as an author. Fix: ICMJE 2026 explicitly bans this; AI cannot bear accountability. Instead flag in Methods / Acknowledgements: "ChatGPT-5 was used for language editing / code prototyping."

對比範例

同一情境，錯與對的寫法

❌ 不及格寫法

Author list：Lee C, Wong A, Liu P.

Acknowledgements：「We thank our lab manager Ms. Chen for collecting tissue samples and running 10x Genomics protocols.」

問題：Ms. Chen 不只是「collecting」——她設計 cell-dissociation 條件、跑 QC、發現 batch effect。實質貢獻符合 ICMJE 條件 ① 與 ②，卻被放在 Acknowledgements——這是 ghost authorship。

Author list: Lee C, Wong A, Liu P.

Acknowledgements: "We thank our lab manager Ms. Chen for collecting tissue samples and running 10x Genomics protocols."

Problem: Ms. Chen didn't just "collect" — she designed dissociation conditions, ran QC, identified batch effects. She meets ICMJE criteria ① and ② but is buried in Acknowledgements: ghost authorship.

✅ 及格寫法

Author list：Lee C, Chen S, Wong A, Liu P.

CRediT statement：「Ms. Chen: Investigation, Methodology, Data Curation, Writing — Review & Editing.」

修正：Ms. Chen 升為 co-author；CRediT 角色明確列出。Acknowledgements 改寫純技術助理（協助 sequencing facility 操作的人員）。符合 ICMJE 4 條件與 transparent contribution 原則。

Author list: Lee C, Chen S, Wong A, Liu P.

CRediT statement: "Ms. Chen: Investigation, Methodology, Data Curation, Writing — Review & Editing."

Fix: Ms. Chen elevated to co-author; CRediT roles listed explicitly. Acknowledgements now lists only true technical helpers (sequencing-facility operators). Meets ICMJE's 4 criteria and the principle of transparent contribution.

反思

投稿前 6 個自問

  1. 你的 author list 中，每一個人都真的有 draft 或 critically review 過嗎？
  2. Did every person on your author list actually draft or critically review?
  3. 每位 co-author 都有 ORCID 嗎？corresponding author 一定要、其他人強烈建議。
  4. Do you have ORCID for every co-author? Mandatory for corresponding, strongly recommended for all.
  5. 同一份 cohort 從這個計畫產生了 >1 篇 paper 嗎？如果是，每篇研究問題與結論實質不同嗎？
  6. Did the same cohort produce more than one paper from this work? If so, do the questions and conclusions substantively differ?
  7. 你的 Methods 段是否引用了 IRB / IACUC approval number 與委員會名稱？

  9. 過去 36 個月內，所有 co-author 是否揭露 funding / consultancy / equity 等 COI？
  10. In the past 36 months, have all co-authors disclosed funding / consultancy / equity COIs?
  11. 本 paper 是否使用 AI 工具？是否在 Methods 或 Acknowledgements 明確揭露？
  12. Did you use any AI tool for this paper? Is it explicitly disclosed in Methods or Acknowledgements?

(c) AI 作者爭議：普遍禁止 + accountability gap

2026 主流期刊（ICMJE / Nature / Science / Cell Press / Springer）共識：AI 不可作為 author。核心理由：

  1. Accountability gap——ICMJE 第 4 條件要求作者「對研究全部負責」。AI 工具（ChatGPT-5, Claude Sonnet 4.6, Gemini 3）無法承擔法律 / 學術責任，因為它沒有法律主體性。
  2. Approval gap——第 3 條件「同意最終版本」無法成立；AI 無法「同意」。
  3. Disclosure 不等於 authorship——AI 使用必須在 Methods / Acknowledgements 揭露（強制），但揭露 ≠ 作者地位。

灰區：AI 共同產生 hypothesis、AI 進行 literature review、AI 撰寫 first draft——這些情境下 AI 的「貢獻」應由負責使用 AI 的人承擔，並在 Methods 揭露 prompts / model versions。

2026 mainstream-publisher consensus (ICMJE / Nature / Science / Cell Press / Springer): AI cannot be an author. Core reasons:

  1. Accountability gap — ICMJE criterion 4 requires authors to be "accountable for all aspects". AI tools (ChatGPT-5, Claude Sonnet 4.6, Gemini 3) cannot bear legal / academic responsibility — they have no legal personhood.
  2. Approval gap — criterion 3 ("approval of final version") cannot be satisfied; AI cannot "approve".
  3. Disclosure ≠ authorship — AI use must be disclosed in Methods / Acknowledgements (mandatory), but disclosure ≠ author status.

Gray zone: AI co-generated hypotheses, AI-run literature reviews, AI-drafted first drafts — the "contribution" should be borne by the human using the AI, with prompts / model versions disclosed in Methods.

歷史軼事：紐倫堡——現代生醫倫理的起點

1948 年的紐倫堡審判（Nazi doctors trial）將 7 位納粹醫師判處死刑——他們在集中營對囚犯進行 hypothermia、高海拔、毒劑等致命人體實驗。隨後在 1947 年宣讀的紐倫堡守則 (Nuremberg Code)制定 10 條原則，其中第 1 條「受試者必須自願同意 (voluntary consent)」成為現代生醫倫理的基石。今日所有 IRB 流程、informed consent 表格、ICMJE 規範，皆可溯源至此。倫理不是被動的合規，它是科學在二戰廢墟之上重建公信力的承諾。

The 1948 Nuremberg trials (Nazi doctors trial) sentenced 7 Nazi physicians to death — they had performed lethal hypothermia, high-altitude, and toxin experiments on concentration-camp prisoners. The Nuremberg Code, read in 1947, laid out 10 principles, of which the first — "voluntary consent of the human subject is absolutely essential" — became the keystone of modern bioethics. Every IRB process, informed-consent form, and ICMJE rule traces back to this moment. Ethics is not passive compliance; it is science's promise to rebuild credibility on the ruins of WWII.

Most ethics issues (IRB, author order, conflicts of interest, past publications) can't be retroactively fixed. Discovered post-submission, the consequences range from reject to retraction + lab blacklist.

2026 required-fillables: (1) ICMJE 4-criteria check for every author; (2) CRediT taxonomy listing per-author contributions; (3) ORCID iD for each author; (4) COI statement.

ORCID 與 ROR

三、ORCID 與 ROR

👤 ORCID iD

16 位數字（如 0000-0002-1825-0097）唯一識別作者。2026 多數主流期刊（PLOS, Nature, Cell, Springer, Wiley）已強制要求 corresponding author 提供 ORCID，且鼓勵共同作者也都提供。

益處：避免姓名同名混淆（特別 Asian author）、自動 sync publication list、整合 funder reports。

A 16-digit unique author identifier (e.g., 0000-0002-1825-0097). By 2026, most mainstream publishers (PLOS, Nature, Cell, Springer, Wiley) require corresponding authors to provide ORCID, with co-authors strongly encouraged.

Benefits: disambiguates same names (especially Asian authors), auto-syncs publications, integrates with funder reports.

🏛️ ROR (Research Organization Registry)

類似 ORCID，但是給 institution 用的全球唯一 ID。例：「National Taiwan University」對應 ROR ID。許多期刊在 metadata 中已內含。

Like ORCID but for institutions — globally unique. E.g., "National Taiwan University" maps to a ROR ID. Many journals embed it in metadata.

行為定義與後果
① 偽造 / 篡改數據 最嚴重的學術不端，會撤稿 + 機構調查 + 終身學術除名。Retraction Watch 上多數案例都源於此。Most serious misconduct — retraction + institutional inquiry + permanent academic ban. Most Retraction Watch cases originate here.

④ Salami Slicing 把一個本應一篇的研究切成多篇 (least publishable units, LPU) 來增加發表數。Editor 會比對 cohort 重疊度。Slicing one study into multiple papers (least publishable units, LPU) to inflate publication count. Editors check cohort overlap.
⑤ Ghost / Gift / Guest Authorship Ghost = 真實貢獻者沒掛名 (常為產業 medical writer)；Gift = 沒貢獻但掛名 (常為老闆 / 系主任)；Guest = 知名學者掛名增加可信度。三者都違反 ICMJE。Ghost = real contributor uncredited (often industry medical writer); Gift = uninvolved person added (boss / chair); Guest = famous name added for credibility. 利益衝突

五、利益衝突 (COI)

2026 ICMJE 推薦的 Disclosure of Interest form 是業界標準。原則：寧可 over-disclose，不要 under-disclose。

需揭露的事項（每位作者）：

  • 本研究相關 funding（含 grant number）
  • 過去 36 個月任何企業的 employment、consultancy、honoraria、stock options、patent、travel support
  • 家屬與本研究相關企業的關係
  • 非金錢的 conflict (advocacy, religion, political)

The 2026 ICMJE Disclosure of Interest form is the industry standard. Principle: over-disclose rather than under-disclose.

To disclose (per author):

  • Funding for this study (including grant numbers)
  • Last 36 months of any corporate employment, consultancy, honoraria, stock options, patents, travel support
  • Family-member relationships with companies relevant to this study
  • Non-financial conflicts (advocacy, religion, political)
