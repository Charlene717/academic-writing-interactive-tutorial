故事開場

場景：博士後 Olivia 與一篇 retracted paper

SCENE · 2025 · Karolinska

從「手敲 references」到「Zotero + Retraction Watch」

博士後 Olivia 在 Karolinska Institutet 寫一篇關於免疫檢查點抑制劑的綜述。她習慣「在 Google Scholar 找到 paper、把資訊複製到 Word reference list 裡手動敲格式」——畢竟「我只引 30 篇，自己排比較快」。

論文投到 Annals of Oncology，三週後收到 reviewer #2 的回信，第一句話就是：「Reference 17 是 2022 年已被 retract 的論文 (因 image manipulation)。請說明為何採用其結論。」 Olivia 嚇出冷汗——那篇是她從 Google Scholar 第一頁複製的，網頁上完全沒有 retraction 標籤。

她連夜學 Zotero：裝瀏覽器 connector、設定 Better BibTeX、串接 Retraction Watch database (Zotero 自動以紅色標示已撤稿論文)、把 30 篇 references 全部用 DOI 重新匯入。一週後 resubmit；reviewer #2 第二輪回覆只有一句：「Refs are now clean. Recommend accept.」

她在實驗室 lab meeting 分享這個教訓，題目寫：「手敲 reference 的錯誤率是 100%——只是你還沒被抓到而已」。

Postdoc Olivia at Karolinska Institutet is writing a review on immune checkpoint inhibitors. She habitually "finds papers on Google Scholar, copies fields into the Word reference list, hand-formats each entry" — after all, "for 30 refs, doing it myself is faster."

The manuscript goes to Annals of Oncology. Three weeks later reviewer #2 opens with: "Reference 17 was retracted in 2022 (image manipulation). Please justify reliance on its conclusions." Olivia goes cold — she'd copied that paper from page one of Google Scholar; the web page showed no retraction tag.

Overnight she learns Zotero: installs the browser connector, configures Better BibTeX, hooks up Retraction Watch integration (Zotero highlights retracted entries in red), and re-imports all 30 references by DOI. One week later she resubmits; reviewer #2's second-round reply is one line: "Refs are now clean. Recommend accept."

She presents the lesson at lab meeting under the title: "The error rate of hand-typed references is 100% — you just haven't been caught yet."

核心詞彙

14 個關鍵術語（雙版本）

每個詞兩個版本：🧒「給小孩看」用比喻、📐「給研究者看」給定義。

Each term has two versions: 🧒 "for kids" uses a metaphor; 📐 "for researchers" gives the formal definition.

Citation
引用
在文章裡指著另一本書說：「這個想法是他先講的」——就像考試寫名字。
A pointer in your text that says "this idea came from there" — like signing whose homework you borrowed.
在 text 中以特定格式 (Vancouver [1] / APA (Smith 2024)) 引用另一作品；對應 reference list 中的完整條目。
A formatted in-text marker (Vancouver [1] / APA (Smith 2024)) pointing to a full entry in the reference list.
Reference list
參考文獻列表
論文最後一頁的「我引了哪些書」清單。
The "books I borrowed from" list at the end of the paper.
論文末完整列出所有引用作品的條目；僅包含 in-text 引用過的；異於 bibliography (含未引用之延伸閱讀)。
The complete list of all cited works at the paper's end; contains only in-text citations; distinct from bibliography (which may include further reading).
Vancouver style
Vancouver 格式
把引用編號 [1][2][3]——像在排隊取號碼牌。
Numbers references [1][2][3] in order of appearance — like a queue ticket.
ICMJE 1978 訂；按文中出現先後編號；NEJM、JAMA、Cell、Nature 系列等大部分生醫期刊使用 (~80%)。
ICMJE 1978; numbered by order of appearance; used by NEJM, JAMA, Cell, Nature, and ~80% of biomedical journals.
APA style
APA 格式
寫作者姓 + 年份 (Smith, 2024) ——一看就知道是誰、什麼時候講的。
Writes (Smith, 2024) — instantly tells you who and when.
American Psychological Association；7th ed (2020)；社會/教育/心理常用；reference list 按字母排序、含 DOI。
American Psychological Association; 7th ed (2020); used in psych/education/social sciences; alphabetical reference list with DOI.
Author-Year (Harvard)
作者-年份（Harvard 風格）
和 APA 很像但細節不同——像兩家茶店都賣珍奶但配方略異。
Similar to APA but with different details — two boba shops, same drink, different recipes.
非單一機構標準；各期刊版本不同 (Harvard Cite Them Right、CSE Name-Year)；生態學/地球科學常用。
No single authority; many variants (Harvard Cite Them Right, CSE Name-Year); common in ecology/earth sciences.
BibTeX
BibTeX
給 LaTeX 看的「引用資料庫檔」——文字檔，肉眼可讀。
A plain-text "reference database" file that LaTeX reads.
Patashnik 1985；.bib 檔；entry types (@article、@book)；fields (author, title, journal, year, doi)；用於 LaTeX。
Patashnik 1985; .bib files; entry types (@article, @book); fields (author, title, journal, year, doi); used with LaTeX.
BibLaTeX
BibLaTeX
BibTeX 的「升級版」，2008 年出來，支援 Unicode、跨語言。
The "upgraded" BibTeX — released 2008, with Unicode and multi-language support.
Lehman 2008；後端用 biber；支援 UTF-8、嵌套 entry、複雜風格；CJK 引用首選。
Lehman 2008; biber backend; UTF-8, nested entries, complex styles; preferred for CJK citations.
CSL
引用樣式語言
用 XML 寫的「格式食譜」——Zotero / Mendeley 用這個自動套各種期刊風格。
An XML "formatting recipe" — Zotero / Mendeley use it to auto-apply journal styles.
Citation Style Language；XML-based；v1.0.2 (2021)；Zotero Style Repository 收錄 10,000+ 期刊樣式。
Citation Style Language; XML-based; v1.0.2 (2021); the Zotero Style Repository holds 10,000+ journal styles.
DOI
數位物件識別碼
論文的「身分證號」——全世界唯一、永久不變。
A paper's permanent ID number — globally unique, never changes.
Digital Object Identifier；ISO 26324；Crossref 1.4B+ 已註冊；前綴/後綴 (10.1038/s41586-024-...)；URL: doi.org/...
Digital Object Identifier; ISO 26324; 1.4B+ registered via Crossref; prefix/suffix format (10.1038/s41586-024-...); URL doi.org/...
PMID
PubMed 編號
PubMed 給每篇生醫論文的「PubMed 內部編號」。
PubMed's internal ID for each biomedical paper.
PubMed Identifier；純整數 (例：38234567)；NLM 維護；PMC ID 為全文版的 ID (PMC10234567)。
PubMed Identifier; integer (e.g., 38234567); maintained by NLM; PMC ID is the full-text version (PMC10234567).
In-text citation
文中引用
在句子裡指著一本書——「就在這句旁邊」。
Pointing at a source right next to a sentence.
論文 body 中插入的引用 marker：[1]、(Smith 2024)、(Smith et al., 2024, p. 12)；對應 reference list 條目。
In-body citation marker: [1], (Smith 2024), (Smith et al., 2024, p. 12); resolves to a reference list entry.
Citation manager
文獻管理軟體
「論文圖書館 + 自動寫引用」的 App——再也不用手敲。
A "paper library + auto-citer" app — no more hand-typing.
Zotero (open-source, free)、EndNote (商業)、Mendeley、Paperpile、JabRef；都支援 BibTeX 匯出與 CSL。
Zotero (open-source, free), EndNote (commercial), Mendeley, Paperpile, JabRef; all support BibTeX export and CSL.
Citation coercion
引用強迫
審查者偷偷說：「你要不要也引一下我的論文？」——學術界的灰色行為。
A reviewer hinting, "How about citing my paper too?" — an academic gray-zone behavior.
Wilhite & Fong 2012 Science；reviewer/editor 要求加引用以提升自己 IF；COPE 視為 misconduct；可向期刊申訴。
Wilhite & Fong 2012 Science; reviewer/editor pressuring extra citations to boost their IF; COPE deems it misconduct; report to journal.
Retraction
撤稿
期刊把一篇論文「收回去」——通常因為造假或重大錯誤。
A journal "takes back" a paper — usually due to fraud or major error.
論文因 misconduct / honest error 被期刊正式撤回；Retraction Watch DB >50,000 條 (2025)；Zotero 可即時警示。
Formal withdrawal of a paper for misconduct / honest error; Retraction Watch DB has >50,000 entries (2025); Zotero can alert you in real time.

Σ 步驟

從 zero 到 publication-ready references

Σ₁
挑文獻管理工具
推薦 Zotero（免費 + 開源 + 跨平台 + 最大社群）。EndNote 適合機構付費用戶；Paperpile 適合 Google Docs 用戶。
Recommended: Zotero (free, open-source, cross-platform, largest community). EndNote suits institutional licenses; Paperpile fits Google Docs users.
Σ₂
裝瀏覽器 connector 與 Word/Quarto plugin
Zotero Connector (Chrome/Firefox/Safari) 一鍵抓 metadata；Word/LibreOffice/Quarto 各有對應 plugin。
Zotero Connector (Chrome/Firefox/Safari) grabs metadata in one click; Word / LibreOffice / Quarto each have a matching plugin.
Σ₃
用 DOI 加 references（避免手動輸入）
Zotero 提供「以識別碼新增」(magic wand 圖示)；貼上 DOI / PMID / ISBN 即可自動抓全部欄位。
Use Zotero's "Add by Identifier" (magic wand icon); paste DOI / PMID / ISBN and all fields auto-populate.
Σ₄
用 collections / tags 分類論文
每篇 paper 一個專案 collection；用 tags 標 method / model / disease；之後可用 saved search 自動歸納。
One project collection per paper; tag by method / model / disease; use saved searches to auto-aggregate later.
Σ₅
設定 Better BibTeX 或 CSL 樣式
LaTeX 用：裝 Better BibTeX plugin → auto-export .bib 檔。Word 用：到 Zotero Style Repository 下載目標期刊 CSL。
For LaTeX: install Better BibTeX plugin → auto-export .bib. For Word: download the target journal's CSL from the Zotero Style Repository.
Σ₆
邊寫邊引（Cite-as-you-write）
Zotero 的 Word/LibreOffice plugin 按 Cmd/Ctrl+Shift+A；輸入幾個關鍵字即可選擇 reference 插入。
In Zotero's Word/LibreOffice plugin press Cmd/Ctrl+Shift+A; type a few keywords to pick and insert a reference.
Σ₇
啟用 Retraction Watch 警示
Zotero 6+ 內建；library 中 retracted papers 以紅色顯示並彈出警示——避免引用到撤稿論文。
Built into Zotero 6+; retracted papers appear in red with a warning popup — preventing accidental citation of retracted works.
Σ₈
產出目標期刊格式
換期刊只需切換 CSL：在 Word plugin 的 Document Preferences 選新樣式，<2 秒重排全部 references。
Switching journals = switching CSL: in Word plugin's Document Preferences pick a new style — all references re-format in <2 s.
專家辯論

3 個值得思考的議題

① Vancouver vs Author-Year：哪個對讀者更友善？
Vancouver 派：編號簡潔、不打斷文流，適合 lab-style paper；Author-Year 派：讀者可立即從 (Smith 2024) 知道是誰何時的研究，不必跳到末頁查 [17]。共識：跟著 target journal——美學偏好不能凌駕格式要求。
Vancouver camp: compact, doesn't break the flow — best for lab-style papers; Author-Year camp: readers know from (Smith 2024) who and when, without flipping to "[17]". Consensus: follow the target journal — aesthetic preference can't override format requirements.
② Reviewer 的「引用強迫」怎麼處理？
判準：如果是合理建議「請考慮加入 Smith 2023 因其與你 method 直接相關」→ 加；如果是「請引用以下 5 篇我的論文，否則拒收」→ COPE 視為 misconduct，可向 editor-in-chief 申訴並要求換 reviewer。
Rule: if "please consider Smith 2023, directly relevant to your method" — add it; if "cite the following 5 of my papers or I'll reject" — COPE considers this misconduct; appeal to editor-in-chief and request a new reviewer.
③ AI 生成的論文要不要被引用？ICMJE 立場為何？
ICMJE 2023-2025：AI 工具（ChatGPT 等）不可列為作者（無法承擔責任）；但 AI-assisted papers 經人類同儕審查後仍可被引用。實務：引用時須能解釋作者 contribution 與 AI 使用範圍。
ICMJE 2023-2025: AI tools (ChatGPT etc.) cannot be listed as authors (cannot bear accountability); AI-assisted papers that pass peer review remain citable. Practical: when citing, be ready to explain author contribution and AI use scope.
聚焦
楷模

Eugene Garfield 1955 Science：引文索引的起點

Garfield 在 Science 122:108-111 (1955) 發表 "Citation Indexes for Science"，提出「以引用關係為基礎建立文獻索引」的概念。此一想法直接孕育了 1960 年的 ISI、之後的 Web of Science、JCR、Impact Factor 全部生態。這是「引用本身值得研究」的起點，也是 bibliometrics 的奠基論文。
Garfield's Science 122:108-111 (1955) paper "Citation Indexes for Science" proposed indexing literature by citation links. The idea directly seeded ISI (1960) and the later Web of Science / JCR / Impact Factor ecosystem — the foundation of bibliometrics, and the moment "citations themselves" became a research object.
警示

Citation cartels：互引炒作 IF 的陷阱

部分期刊（與其作者群）形成「互引集團」(citation cartel) ——彼此頻繁引用以人為拉高 IF。Clarivate 自 2012 年起每年公布 JCR 「Suppressed Journals」名單，將異常自引/互引的期刊暫停刊載 IF；2024 名單超過 50 本。投稿前 PI 應檢查目標期刊是否曾被 suppress。
Some journals (and their author networks) form "citation cartels" — citing each other heavily to inflate IF. Since 2012 Clarivate publishes an annual JCR "Suppressed Journals" list pausing IF reporting for outliers in self/inter-citation; the 2024 list exceeds 50 journals. Check target journals' suppression history before submission.
陷阱

6 個典型陷阱

1
一句話引 10 篇 [1-10]
非具體；讀者無法分辨各篇貢獻。改為「Smith 2022 提出 X，Lin 2023 用單細胞驗證 Y」。
Not specific; readers can't tell each paper's contribution. Replace with "Smith 2022 proposed X; Lin 2023 validated Y with single-cell".
2
手敲 BibTeX 條目有錯字
Author 名漏字、year 打錯、doi 缺一碼——LaTeX compile 不報錯但 reviewer 一抓就被打回。一律從 manager 匯出。
Missing letters in author name, wrong year, doi off by one digit — LaTeX won't complain, reviewers will. Always export from a manager.
3
同一篇論文混用兩種風格
前半 Vancouver、後半 APA——通常因為換 supervisor 或合作者直接貼上自己的段落。檢查方法：grep "et al" 與 "[" 共存代表混格式。
First half Vancouver, second half APA — usually after a supervisor swap or a collaborator pasting their own section. Check by grep'ing both "et al" and "[" in the same file.
4
只引自己 group 的論文
引用 list 90% 同一 PI——reviewer 立刻察覺；顯得不夠了解 field。每章節至少 3 個外部 group。
90% of references share the same PI — reviewers spot it instantly; signals weak field awareness. At least 3 outside groups per section.
5
引用 retracted papers 不自知
Google Scholar 不顯示 retraction；PubMed 才會。Zotero 6+ 內建 Retraction Watch 警示——投稿前掃一遍。
Google Scholar doesn't flag retractions; PubMed does. Zotero 6+ has built-in Retraction Watch alerts — sweep before submission.
6
從 Google Scholar 複製貼上 references 沒驗證
Scholar 的 cite 按鈕 metadata 常有錯（缺 issue、頁碼、DOI）。用 Zotero connector 抓原始 publisher 頁面才準確。
Scholar's cite button often has wrong metadata (missing issue, pages, DOI). Use Zotero connector to grab the publisher page instead.
對比

具體 vs 模糊引用

不好
「許多研究顯示 scRNA-seq 在腫瘤學中很有用 [1-15]。」
"Many studies have shown that scRNA-seq is useful [1-15]."
15 篇糊在一起，無法分辨各篇貢獻；reviewer 會問「具體哪些 paper、哪些發現？」
15 papers lumped — reader can't tell each contribution; reviewer will ask "which papers, which findings?"
好
「先驅性 scRNA-seq 在 TNBC 的圖譜研究 (Wu 等 2021；Bassez 等 2021) 描繪了腫瘤間異質性，但化療期間的縱向細胞態變化尚未被探索。」
"Pioneering scRNA-seq atlases of TNBC (Wu et al. 2021; Bassez et al. 2021) characterized inter-tumoral heterogeneity, but the longitudinal cell-state changes during chemotherapy remain unexplored."
2 篇關鍵 paper 點名 + 各篇貢獻 + 留下 gap → 自然連到自己的研究問題。
Two key papers named + each contribution + gap left → naturally leads into your research question.
自我反思

6 個自我檢核問題

  1. 你最近是否曾換過某篇論文的目標期刊？換 CSL 樣式花了多久？
  2. 你引用論文時是「具體點名」還是「整段塞 [1-10]」？
  3. 你的 reference list 是否避免了 self-citation spam？
  4. 你的 Zotero / EndNote 是否啟用 Retraction Watch 警示？
  5. 若 reviewer 暗示「請引用我的 5 篇 paper」，你會如何回應？
  6. 你的下一篇論文有沒有計畫加 ORCID 與 DOI 連結？
進階

2024-2026 SOTA 進階主題

(a) Citation Style Language (CSL) v1.0.2 schema 解析
CSL 1.0.2 (2021) 為 XML 標準，三大部分：<info>（樣式 metadata：作者、title、自我描述）、<macro>（可重用引用片段，如 author-short、year-suffix）、<citation> 與 <bibliography>（in-text 與末頁排版規則）。每個 <layout> 可用 <text>、<date>、<number>、<names> 組合。Zotero Style Repository 提供 10,000+ 樣式；可從 visual editor (citationstyles.org) 線上編輯。
CSL 1.0.2 (2021) is an XML standard with three parts: <info> (style metadata: author, title, self-description), <macro> (reusable citation snippets like author-short, year-suffix), and <citation> + <bibliography> (in-text + end-of-paper layout rules). Each <layout> composes <text>, <date>, <number>, <names> elements. The Zotero Style Repository ships 10,000+ styles; edit via the visual editor at citationstyles.org.
(b) Better BibTeX for Zotero：LaTeX 寫作必裝
Emiliano Heyns 開發的 Zotero plugin：①「Pin citation key」確保 BibTeX key 跨機器穩定（如 smith2024scrna）；② Auto-export：library 變動時自動更新 .bib；③ Citation key formula 客製化（auth.lower + year + shorttitle）；④ 支援 BibLaTeX、biblatex-chicago。LaTeX + Overleaf 用戶幾乎必裝；2025 年下載 10M+。
A Zotero plugin by Emiliano Heyns: (1) "Pin citation key" keeps BibTeX keys stable across machines (e.g., smith2024scrna); (2) auto-export refreshes .bib on library change; (3) customizable citation-key formula (auth.lower + year + shorttitle); (4) supports BibLaTeX and biblatex-chicago. Near-mandatory for LaTeX + Overleaf users; 10M+ downloads by 2025.
(c) Scite.ai：2025-26 的引用脈絡分析工具
Scite.ai 用 NLP 把每筆引用分類為 supporting / contrasting / mentioning。截至 2025 已分析 1.2B+ 引用 context。實務用法：①寫 review 時找出哪些 paper 真的支持你的論點而非僅 mention；② identifying "controversial" papers (高 contrasting 引用比例)；③ Zotero 整合可在 library 中即時顯示。限制：模型對非英文與 chemistry/math 領域精度仍待提升。
Scite.ai uses NLP to classify each citation as supporting / contrasting / mentioning. As of 2025 it has classified 1.2B+ citation contexts. Use cases: (1) when writing a review, find papers truly supporting your claim vs just mentioning; (2) identify "controversial" papers (high contrasting ratio); (3) Zotero integration shows status inline. Caveats: accuracy still weaker for non-English and chemistry/math.
歷史小記

Eugene Garfield：被 4 個期刊拒絕的奠基論文

1955 年 Eugene Garfield 在 Science 發表的 "Citation Indexes for Science" 之前，曾被 4 個期刊拒絕——審查者認為「引用本身不值得索引」。Garfield 不放棄；最終 Science 接受發表後，他在 1960 年於費城創立 Institute for Scientific Information (ISI)，推出 Science Citation Index (1964)、Journal Citation Reports、Impact Factor。ISI 後來經 Thomson Reuters 易手，2016 年成為今天的 Clarivate。一篇被連退 4 次的論文，最後改變了整個學術評量體系——這也是「審查意見不等於真理」最經典的歷史證據。
Before Eugene Garfield's 1955 Science paper "Citation Indexes for Science" was accepted, it had been rejected by 4 other journals — reviewers thought "citations themselves are not worth indexing". Garfield persisted; once Science published it, he founded the Institute for Scientific Information (ISI) in Philadelphia (1960), launching the Science Citation Index (1964), Journal Citation Reports, and the Impact Factor. ISI later passed through Thomson Reuters and in 2016 became today's Clarivate. A paper rejected four times reshaped the entire academic-evaluation ecosystem — perhaps the most iconic historical proof that "reviewer opinion is not truth".

為什麼引用比想像中重要

引用做不好，論文會在三個地方被打回票：(1) Editor desk-reject 因為格式錯太多；(2) Reviewer 抓你「沒引用 obvious 的關鍵 paper」(常常是 reviewer 自己的論文)；(3) Production 階段被製作部要求重做整份 reference list。

2026 年的標準工作流：用工具管理 (Zotero / EndNote / Paperpile)，用 CSL 自動套格式，DOI + ORCID 必填。手寫 reference 已是上一代做法。

Bad citations get rejected in three places: (1) editor desk-reject for too many formatting errors; (2) reviewer flags "missing obvious key paper" (often their own); (3) production team kicks the manuscript back demanding a full reference rebuild.

The 2026 workflow: manage with a tool (Zotero / EndNote / Paperpile), auto-format via CSL, DOI + ORCID required. Hand-typing references is a previous-generation practice.

CSL 客製化

三、CSL 客製化

CSL (Citation Style Language) 是業界標準 XML 格式，由 Mendeley 與 Zotero 共同維護。Zotero Style Repository 提供超過 10000 種期刊樣式，幾乎涵蓋所有 SCI/SSCI 期刊。

典型工作流：

  1. Zotero 建立 collection，把要引的論文拖進去
  2. 在 Word/LibreOffice 安裝 Zotero plugin
  3. 「Document Preferences → Style」選擇目標期刊（如 "Nature Medicine"）
  4. 插入引用 → 文末按 "Insert Bibliography" 自動生成
  5. 改投另一期刊？只需要切換 Style，自動全篇重排

CSL (Citation Style Language) is the industry-standard XML format jointly maintained by Mendeley and Zotero. The Zotero Style Repository hosts 10000+ journal styles covering virtually all SCI/SSCI journals.

Typical workflow:

  1. Create a Zotero collection; drag papers in.
  2. Install the Zotero Word/LibreOffice plugin.
  3. Document Preferences → Style → pick target journal (e.g., "Nature Medicine").
  4. Insert citations → click "Insert Bibliography" at the end.
  5. Switching journals? Just change the Style — the whole paper reformats automatically.
範例對照

四、in-text 引用對照

❌ 籠統

"Many studies have shown that single-cell technology is useful in cancer research [1-15]."
(這 15 篇是哪些？讀者要怎麼追？「many studies」沒有指向性。)

"Many studies have shown that single-cell technology is useful in cancer research [1-15]."
(Which 15 papers? How does the reader follow up? "Many studies" is non-specific.)

✅ 具體 + 對話

"Pioneering scRNA-seq atlases of TNBC have characterized inter-tumoral heterogeneity (Wu et al. 2021; Bassez et al. 2021), but the longitudinal cell-state changes during chemotherapy remain unexplored."

"Pioneering scRNA-seq atlases of TNBC have characterized inter-tumoral heterogeneity (Wu et al., 2021; Bassez et al., 2021), but the longitudinal cell-state changes during chemotherapy remain unexplored."

❌ 自我引用過量

「我們先前做了 X (Lee 2020, Lee 2021, Lee 2022, Lee 2023)，這次延伸到 Y。」(自引 4 篇且都 first-author)

"We previously did X (Lee 2020; Lee 2021; Lee 2022; Lee 2023) and now extend to Y." (4 self-cites, all first-author)

✅ 節制 + 必要

「我們先前建立 cell atlas (Lee et al. 2023)，本研究延伸到 chemotherapy 介入後的時間點。」

"We previously established a cell atlas (Lee et al., 2023); here we extend to post-chemotherapy time points."

chemotherapy response.

\printbibliography
