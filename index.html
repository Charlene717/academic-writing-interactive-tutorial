從研究問題到期刊發表——14 個主題帶你掌握生醫/生資領域碩博士論文與 SCI/SSCI 期刊論文的完整寫作與發表流程，搭配範例對照、決策樹與互動模擬。

From research question to journal publication — master thesis writing and SCI/SSCI publishing in biomedical/bioinformatics across 14 modules, with bad-vs-good examples, decision trees, and interactive simulators.

Use PICO / FINER frameworks to refine vague interests into testable hypotheses. Distinguish aim, objective, and hypothesis.

Step 2

When to use narrative vs systematic vs scoping reviews. PubMed/MeSH search strategies and PRISMA flow.

Step 3

Journal IMRaD vs 5-chapter / 6-chapter / paper-based thesis structures — when to choose which.

Step 4

CARS model (Create A Research Space): establish territory → create gap → occupy gap. Funnel paragraph structure.

Step 5

方法寫作

EQUATOR/STROBE/CONSORT/ARRIVE checklist；軟體版本、code/data availability、ethics statement。

EQUATOR/STROBE/CONSORT/ARRIVE checklists; software versions, code/data availability, ethics statements.

Step 6

Figure design principles, Table 1 conventions, full effect-size + 95% CI reporting, common statistical pitfalls.

Step 7

Inverted funnel, dialogue with prior literature, honest limitations, avoid overclaiming.

Step 8

STORY OF THE TUTORIAL

從模糊主題到可發表論文 / From a vague topic to a publishable paper

起點只是「我想做 TNBC 三陰性乳癌研究」一句模糊敘述。透過 14 步驟把研究問題、IMRaD 結構、緒論的 CARS、方法的 EQUATOR、結果的效應量、討論的限制誠實揭露、摘要 SEO、引用規範、期刊選擇、回覆審稿到 AI 揭露逐一磨亮，最後成為一篇能投得進 SCI 的論文。每章開場故事都從這個案例延伸。

It started as a vague line — "I want to study TNBC." Across 14 steps the research question, IMRaD scaffold, CARS introduction, EQUATOR methods, full effect-size results, honest limitations, SEO abstract, citation rules, journal choice, response letter, and AI disclosure are each polished until it becomes a publishable SCI paper. Every chapter's opening story extends this case.

🏆
MODERN PUBLISHING 2026

2026 的現代發表流程長什麼樣？ / What modern publishing looks like

一篇 2026 的論文流程：idea → bioRxiv preprint 先佔位 → 在 Quarto / R Markdown 寫 AI-aware 且可重現的 draft，code+data 同步 deposit (GitHub + Zenodo DOI) → 投稿時揭露 generative AI 使用 → 經 peer review → accept → 以 ORCID + ROR 追蹤作者與機構。整套都在 Step 11~14 解析。

A 2026 paper goes from idea → bioRxiv preprint → AI-aware reproducible Quarto/R Markdown draft (code+data deposited to GitHub + Zenodo DOI) → submission with generative-AI disclosure → peer review → accept → ORCID/ROR-tracked. Steps 11-14 unpack the full chain.

If this is your first thesis or journal paper, read Step 1 through Step 14 in order. Chapters cross-reference each other and a sequential pass builds the full mental model.
