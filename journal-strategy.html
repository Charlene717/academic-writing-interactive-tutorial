STEP 11 / 14

期刊選擇與投稿策略

投高、投對、投快——三者只能選二。如何用 scope/IF/受眾/OA 四維度做戰略選擇，並避開掠奪性期刊陷阱。

Aim high, aim right, aim fast — pick two. Use scope/IF/audience/OA to choose strategically, and dodge predatory journals.

概覽

投稿是策略遊戲

許多人投稿失敗不是因為論文不好，而是因為投錯期刊。錯誤的期刊會在 24 小時內 desk-reject (editor 一看就知道不適合)，浪費你 3-6 個月找下一家。

正確策略：在寫之前就鎖定 1 個 first-choice + 2 個 backup，並照那家期刊的 author guidelines 寫。等寫完才挑期刊，注定要返工。

Many submissions fail not because of poor writing but because of poor journal fit. Wrong journal = desk-reject in 24 hours, costing 3-6 months looking for the next.

Correct strategy: lock in 1 first-choice + 2 backups before writing, and follow that journal's author guidelines. Picking a journal after writing means rework.

💡
Pre-submission inquiry：對 high-IF 期刊 (Nature, Cell, Lancet 等)，可在投稿前發 1 段 email 摘要到 editorial office 詢問 fit。Editor 通常 1-2 週內回覆「encourage to submit / not a good fit」，能避免你白寫一個版本的 cover letter。 Pre-submission inquiry: for high-IF journals (Nature, Cell, Lancet), email a 1-paragraph summary to the editorial office before submitting. Editors typically reply within 1-2 weeks with "encourage to submit / not a good fit" — saves a wasted submission cycle.

OA 四模式

二、Open Access 四模式

🥇 Gold OA

作者付 APC ($1500-$11000)，論文立即免費公開。例：PLOS ONE, Nature Communications, BMC, Scientific Reports。NIH/Wellcome funder 偏好。

Author pays APC ($1500-$11000), paper immediately open. E.g., PLOS ONE, Nature Communications, BMC, Scientific Reports. Preferred by NIH/Wellcome.

🟢 Green OA

傳統 paywall 但允許作者把 accepted manuscript 自存到 institutional repository / bioRxiv / PubMed Central（通常 6-12 月 embargo）。

Traditional paywall but author can self-archive the accepted manuscript to institutional repository / bioRxiv / PubMed Central (often 6-12 month embargo).

🟡 Hybrid OA

傳統訂閱期刊 + 「付費可選 OA」($2000-$5000)。被 cOAlition S 與 Plan S 視為「double-dipping」而排斥。

Subscription journal + "optional pay-for-OA" ($2000-$5000). Considered "double-dipping" by cOAlition S / Plan S.

💎 Diamond OA

免費投稿、免費讀。由學會、機構、聯盟資助。例：eLife (post-2023), Wellcome Open Research, F1000Research。對 LMIC 作者特別友善。

Free to publish, free to read. Funded by societies / institutions / consortia. E.g., eLife (post-2023), Wellcome Open Research, F1000Research. Especially friendly to LMIC authors.

掠奪性期刊

三、避開掠奪性期刊

掠奪性期刊 (predatory journals) 收費快、審查空轉、學術記錄無保障。投了不只浪費 APC，還會被嚴肅期刊視為 "predatory record" 而拒收後續投稿。

2026 年驗證工具：Cabells Predatory Reports (付費精準) + Think.Check.Submit. (免費 checklist) + DOAJ (Directory of Open Access Journals 白名單)。

Predatory journals collect fees fast, run sham peer review, and offer no archival guarantees. Submitting wastes APC and creates a "predatory record" that mainstream journals reject in future.

2026 verification: Cabells Predatory Reports (paid, precise), Think.Check.Submit. (free checklist), DOAJ (Directory of Open Access Journals — whitelist).

🚩 主動邀稿、語氣浮誇

"Esteemed Researcher, We are highly impressed by your work..." — 真期刊不會主動寄這種信。

"Esteemed Researcher, We are highly impressed by your work..." — real journals don't cold-email like this.

🚩 7 天就能完成 peer review

正常 peer review 至少 4-12 週。承諾「rapid 7 天」幾乎一定是空轉。

Real peer review takes 4-12 weeks. "Rapid 7 days" is a near-certain sham.

🚩 編輯團隊不透明

沒列 editor 全名 / 機構，或列了但對方否認 (常被冒用)。Google 一下 editor 的姓名 + 期刊名。

No editor names/affiliations listed, or listed people deny involvement (often impersonated). Google editor name + journal name.

🚩 網站不專業

排版亂、圖片低解析、英文文法錯多、模仿大期刊命名 ("Lancet of XXX")。

Sloppy layout, low-res images, broken English, big-journal-mimicking name ("Lancet of XXX").

🚩 不在 PubMed/Scopus/DOAJ

真期刊一定被至少一個主要 indexing service 收錄。OA 期刊未在 DOAJ → 100% 警示。

Real journals appear in at least one major index. OA journal not in DOAJ → 100% red flag.

🚩 假 IF / 自製 metric

"Universal Impact Factor 8.5" — Clarivate 才發 IF。任何不是 JCR 的「IF」都是假的。

"Universal Impact Factor 8.5" — only Clarivate issues IF. Any non-JCR "IF" is fake.
